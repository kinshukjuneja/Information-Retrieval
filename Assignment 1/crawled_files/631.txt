a <a href="/wiki/Non-metropolitan_district" title="Non-metropolitan district">local government district</a> with <a href="/wiki/Borough" title="Borough">borough</a> status in <a href="/wiki/Somerset" title="Somerset">Somerset</a>, England.  Its council is based in <a href="/wiki/Taunton" title="Taunton">Taunton</a>.\n</p><p>The district was formed on 1 April 1974, under the <a href="/wiki/Local_Government_Act_1972" title="Local Government Act 1972">Local Government Act 1972</a>, by a merger of the <a href="/wiki/Taunton" title="Taunton">Municipal Borough of Taunton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wellington,_Somerset" title="Wellington, Somerset">Wellington Urban District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Taunton_Rural_District" title="Taunton Rural District">Taunton Rural District</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Wellington_Rural_District_(Somerset)" title="Wellington Rural District (Somerset)">Wellington Rural District</a>.\n</p><p>Taunton Deane was granted <a href="/wiki/Borough_status_in_England_and_Wales" class="mw-redirect" title="Borough status in England and Wales">borough status</a> in 1975, perpetuating the <a href="/wiki/Mayor" title="Mayor">mayoralty</a> of Taunton.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>The district was given the name of an alternative form of the <a href="/wiki/Taunton_Deane_(hundred)" class="mw-redirect" title="Taunton Deane (hundred)">Taunton Deane Hundred</a>.\n</p><p>In September 2016, <a href="/wiki/West_Somerset" title="West Somerset">West Somerset</a> and Taunton Deane councils agreed in principle to merge the districts into one (with one council) subject to consultation.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The new district would not be a unitary authority, with Somerset County Council still performing its functions.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> In March 2018 both councils voted in favour of the merger and it will come into effect on 1st April 2019, with the first elections to the new council in May 2019. The new authority will be known as <a href="/wiki/Somerset_West_and_Taunton" title="Somerset West and Taunton">Somerset West and Taunton</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a
