id="Boundaries">Boundaries</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=South_West_England_(European_Parliament_constituency)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Boundaries">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The constituency consists of the <a href="/wiki/South_West_England" title="South West England">South West England</a> region of the United Kingdom, comprising the <a href="/wiki/Ceremonial_counties_of_England" title="Ceremonial counties of England">ceremonial counties</a> of <a href="/wiki/Bristol" title="Bristol">Bristol</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cornwall" title="Cornwall">Cornwall</a>, <a href="/wiki/Devon" title="Devon">Devon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dorset" title="Dorset">Dorset</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gloucestershire" title="Gloucestershire">Gloucestershire</a>, <a href="/wiki/Somerset" title="Somerset">Somerset</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wiltshire" title="Wiltshire">Wiltshire</a>. It has also included the <a href="/wiki/British_overseas_territories" class="mw-redirect" title="British overseas territories">British overseas territory</a> of <a href="/wiki/Gibraltar" title="Gibraltar">Gibraltar</a> <a href="/wiki/European_Parliament_election,_2004_(Gibraltar)" title="European Parliament election, 2004 (Gibraltar)">since 2004</a>.\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=South_West_England_(European_Parliament_constituency)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The constituency was formed as a result of the <a href="/wiki/European_Parliamentary_Elections_Act_1999" title="European Parliamentary Elections Act 1999">European Parliamentary Elections Act 1999</a>, replacing a number of single-member constituencies. These were <a href="/wiki/Bristol_(European_Parliament_constituency)" title="Bristol (European Parliament constituency)">Bristol</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cornwall_and_West_Plymouth_(European_Parliament_constituency)" title="Cornwall and West Plymouth (European Parliament constituency)">Cornwall and West Plymouth</a>, <a href="/wiki/Devon_and_East_Plymouth_(European_Parliament_constituency)" title="Devon and East Plymouth (European Parliament constituency)">Devon and East Plymouth</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dorset_and_East_Devon_(European_Parliament_constituency)" title="Dorset and East Devon (European Parliament constituency)">Dorset and East Devon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Somerset_and_North_Devon_(European_Parliament_constituency)" title="Somerset and North Devon (European Parliament constituency)">Somerset and North Devon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wiltshire_North_and_Bath_(European_Parliament_constituency)" title="Wiltshire North and Bath (European Parliament constituency)">Wiltshire North and Bath</a>, and parts of <a href="/wiki/Cotswolds_(European_Parliament_constituency)" title="Cotswolds (European Parliament constituency)">Cotswolds</a>.\n</p><p>Before the <a href="/wiki/European_Parliament_election,_2004_(United_Kingdom)" title="European Parliament election, 2004 (United Kingdom)">2004 election</a>, it was expanded to include <a href="/wiki/Gibraltar" title="Gibraltar">Gibraltar</a>. This was the result of a 1999 <a href="/wiki/European_Court_of_Human_Rights" title="European Court of Human Rights">European Court of Human Rights</a> case, which argued that Gibraltar should be entitled to vote in European elections. Spain took a complaint about Gibraltar participating in European elections to the <a href="/wiki/European_Court_of_Justice" title="European Court of Justice">European Court of Justice</a>, but their case was unsuccessful.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<table class="wikitable">\n\n<tbody><tr>\n<th colspan="10">MEPs for former South West
