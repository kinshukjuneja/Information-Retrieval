a heavily polluted river in <a href="/wiki/Cornwall" title="Cornwall">Cornwall</a>, England.<sup id="cite_ref-restore_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-restore-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It starts in <a href="/wiki/Chacewater" title="Chacewater">Chacewater</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/w/index.php?title=Trewedna_Water&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Trewedna Water (page does not exist)">Trewedna Water</a><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/w/index.php?title=River_Kennall&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="River Kennall (page does not exist)">River Kennall</a><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> flow into the Carnon before it merges with <a href="/w/index.php?title=Tallack%27s_Creek&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Tallack&#39;s Creek (page does not exist)">Tallack\'s Creek</a> to become <a href="/wiki/Restronguet_Creek" title="Restronguet Creek">Restronguet Creek</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> which eventually flows into <a href="/wiki/English_Channel" title="English Channel">the English Channel</a> at the mouth of <a href="/wiki/Carrick_Roads" title="Carrick Roads">Carrick Roads</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Nebra_sky_disc" class="mw-redirect" title="Nebra sky disc">Nebra sky disc</a>, a gold-decorated bronze disc found in Germany and dated to the <a href="/wiki/Bronze_Age" title="Bronze Age">Bronze Age</a> contains both gold and tin from the Carnon valley.<sup id="cite_ref-Ehser_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Ehser-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>In 1992 the river was hit by a major pollution incident, when over 45 million litres of <a href="/wiki/Acid_mine_drainage" title="Acid mine drainage">contaminated water</a> from the closed <a href="/wiki/Wheal_Jane" title="Wheal Jane">Wheal Jane</a> mine was released by the collapse of an <a href="/wiki/Adit" title="Adit">adit</a>, colouring the river water red. A treatment works has since been installed at Wheal Jane to intercept the contaminated water and treat it to remove suspended metals and restore a neutral pH.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCPump_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCPump-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="See_also">See also</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Carnon_River&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: See also">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<ul><li><a href="/wiki/List_of_rivers_in_Cornwall" title="List of rivers in Cornwall">List of rivers in Cornwall</a></li>\n<li><a href="/wiki/List_of_rivers_of_England" title="List of rivers of England">List of rivers of England</a></li></ul>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="References">References</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Carnon_River&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: References">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<div class="reflist" style="list-style-type: decimal;">\n<div class="mw-references-wrap"><ol class="references">\n<li id="cite_note-restore-1"><span class="mw-cite-backlink"><b><a href="#cite_ref-restore_1-0">^</a></b></span> <span class="reference-text"><cite class="citation web"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://restorerivers.eu/wiki/index.php?title=Case_study%3ACarnon_River%3A_Abandoned_Metal_Mines">"Case study:Carnon River: Abandoned Metal Mines"</a>. <i>Restore</i>.
