href="/wiki/Area_of_Outstanding_Natural_Beauty" title="Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty">Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty</a> in <a href="/wiki/Suffolk" title="Suffolk">Suffolk</a>, England.\n</p><p>The AONB covers ancient woodland, commercial forestry, the estuaries of the <a href="/wiki/River_Alde" title="River Alde">Alde</a>, <a href="/wiki/River_Blyth,_Suffolk" title="River Blyth, Suffolk">Blyth</a>, <a href="/wiki/Deben_River" class="mw-redirect" title="Deben River">Deben</a>, <a href="/wiki/River_Orwell" title="River Orwell">Orwell</a> and <a href="/wiki/River_Stour,_Suffolk" title="River Stour, Suffolk">Stour</a> rivers, farmland, <a href="/wiki/Salt_marsh" title="Salt marsh">salt marsh</a>, <a href="/wiki/Heathland" class="mw-redirect" title="Heathland">heathland</a>, mudflats, <a href="/wiki/Reed_bed" title="Reed bed">reed beds</a>, small towns and villages, <a href="/wiki/Shingle_beach" title="Shingle beach">shingle beaches</a> and low eroding cliffs along 60 miles of coastline.\n</p><p>Features include the coastal towns of <a href="/wiki/Aldeburgh" title="Aldeburgh">Aldeburgh</a> and <a href="/wiki/Southwold" title="Southwold">Southwold</a>. <a href="/wiki/Bawdsey" title="Bawdsey">Bawdsey</a>, <a href="/wiki/Covehithe" title="Covehithe">Covehithe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dunwich" title="Dunwich">Dunwich</a>, <a href="/wiki/Minsmere" title="Minsmere">Minsmere</a>, <a href="/wiki/Orford,_Suffolk" title="Orford, Suffolk">Orford</a>, <a href="/wiki/Orford_Ness" title="Orford Ness">Orford Ness</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sizewell" title="Sizewell">Sizewell</a>, <a href="/wiki/Thorpeness" title="Thorpeness">Thorpeness</a>, <a href="/wiki/Walberswick" title="Walberswick">Walberswick</a> and the <a href="/wiki/RSPB_Minsmere" title="RSPB Minsmere">RSPB Minsmere</a> Reserve. There are three National Nature Reserves in the area and many <a href="/wiki/Sites_of_Special_Scientific_Interest" class="mw-redirect" title="Sites of Special Scientific Interest">Sites of Special Scientific Interest</a>. Three long distance footpaths pass through the AONB: the <a href="/wiki/Suffolk_Coast_Path" title="Suffolk Coast Path">Suffolk Coast Path</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Sandlings_Walk" title="Sandlings Walk">Sandlings Walk</a> and the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Stour_and_Orwell_Walk&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Stour and Orwell Walk (page does not exist)">Stour and Orwell Walk</a>.\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="See_also">See also</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a
