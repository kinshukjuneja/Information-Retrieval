href="/wiki/Borough" title="Borough">borough</a> in <a href="/wiki/Devon" title="Devon">Devon</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>.  Towns in the district include <a href="/wiki/Chagford" title="Chagford">Chagford</a>, <a href="/wiki/Okehampton" title="Okehampton">Okehampton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Princetown" title="Princetown">Princetown</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Tavistock,_Devon" class="mw-redirect" title="Tavistock, Devon">Tavistock</a>, where the council is based.\n</p><p>The district was formed on 1 April 1974, under the <a href="/wiki/Local_Government_Act_1972" title="Local Government Act 1972">Local Government Act 1972</a>, as a merger of the previous <a href="/wiki/Municipal_borough" title="Municipal borough">municipal borough</a> of Okehampton, <a href="/wiki/Okehampton" title="Okehampton">Okehampton Rural District</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Tavistock,_Devon" class="mw-redirect" title="Tavistock, Devon">Tavistock Rural District</a>.
