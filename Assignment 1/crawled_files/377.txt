-6.2826</span></span></span></a></span></span></span></td></tr></tbody></table>\n<p><b>Higher Town</b> is the easternmost and largest settlement on the island of <a href="/wiki/St_Martin%27s,_Isles_of_Scilly" title="St Martin&#39;s, Isles of Scilly">St Martin\'s</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Isles_of_Scilly" title="Isles of Scilly">Isles of Scilly</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. It is situated just inland, though the more modern of the island\'s two <a href="/wiki/Quay" class="mw-redirect" title="Quay">quays</a> is located just south of the settlement<sup id="cite_ref-ordnance_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ordnance-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and is named Higher Town Quay.\n</p><p>A bakery, <i>St Martin\'s Bakery</i>,<sup id="cite_ref-Bakery_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Bakery-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> is located at Higher Town. The island\'s main shop and only <a href="/wiki/Post_office" title="Post office">post office</a> is also located in the settlement,<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> as is the <i>Polreath Tea Rooms</i>.\n</p><p>The settlement has two churches (and they are the only churches on the island) - the <a href="/wiki/Church_of_England" title="Church of England">Church of England</a> <a href="/wiki/St_Martin%27s_Church,_St_Martin%27s" title="St Martin&#39;s Church, St Martin&#39;s">parish church of St Martin\'s</a>, and also a <a href="/wiki/Methodism" title="Methodism">Methodist</a> chapel (one of two in the Isles of Scilly) which dates from about 1845.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> The island\'s fire and <a href="/wiki/Her_Majesty%27s_Coastguard" title="Her Majesty&#39;s Coastguard">coastguard</a> station is situated by the CofE parish church.\n</p><p>The highest <a href="/wiki/Elevation" title="Elevation">elevation</a> of the town (though not the island as a whole) is near the post office, at 32 metres (105&#160;ft) above sea level.<sup id="cite_ref-ordnance_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ordnance-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="St_Agnes">St Agnes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Higher_Town,_Isles_of_Scilly&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: St Agnes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>There is also a small settlement called Higher Town on the island of <a href="/wiki/St_Agnes,_Isles_of_Scilly" title="St Agnes, Isles of Scilly">St Agnes</a>.
