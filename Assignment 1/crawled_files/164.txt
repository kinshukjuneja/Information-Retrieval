February 1969) is a former English <a href="/wiki/Cricket" title="Cricket">cricketer</a>. He was a right-handed <a href="/wiki/Batsman_(cricket)" class="mw-redirect" title="Batsman (cricket)">batsman</a> who bowled right-arm <a href="/wiki/Seam_bowling" title="Seam bowling">medium pace</a>. He was born in <a href="/wiki/Hugh_Town" title="Hugh Town">Hugh Town</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Isles_of_Scilly" title="Isles of Scilly">Isles of Scilly</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cornwall" title="Cornwall">Cornwall</a>.\n</p><p>Bell made his debut in <a href="/wiki/County_cricket" title="County cricket">county cricket</a> for <a href="/wiki/Cornwall_County_Cricket_Club" title="Cornwall County Cricket Club">Cornwall</a> in the 1990 <a href="/wiki/MCCA_Knockout_Trophy" title="MCCA Knockout Trophy">MCCA Knockout Trophy</a> against <a href="/wiki/Wiltshire_County_Cricket_Club" title="Wiltshire County Cricket Club">Wiltshire</a>. In that same season he made his <a href="/wiki/First-class_cricket" title="First-class cricket">first-class</a> debut for <a href="/wiki/Gloucestershire_County_Cricket_Club" title="Gloucestershire County Cricket Club">Gloucestershire</a> against <a href="/wiki/Glamorgan_County_Cricket_Club" title="Glamorgan County Cricket Club">Glamorgan</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990_County_Championship" title="1990 County Championship">1990 County Championship</a>. He made two further first-class appearances, against <a href="/wiki/Worcestershire_County_Cricket_Club" title="Worcestershire County Cricket Club">Worcestershire</a> in 1990 and <a href="/wiki/Oxford_University_Cricket_Club" title="Oxford University Cricket Club">Oxford University</a> in 1991.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>  In his three first-class matches, he took 3 wickets at an <a href="/wiki/Bowling_average" title="Bowling average">average</a> of 41.00, with best figures of 2/38.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>  Bell also made a single <a href="/wiki/List_A_cricket" title="List A cricket">List A</a> appearance for Gloucestershire, which came in 1990 against <a href="/wiki/Yorkshire_County_Cricket_Club" title="Yorkshire County Cricket Club">Yorkshire</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1990_Refuge_Assurance_League" title="1990 Refuge Assurance League">Refuge Assurance League</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>  He continued to play <a href="/wiki/Minor_counties_of_English_and_Welsh_cricket" title="Minor counties of English and Welsh cricket">Minor counties</a> cricket for Cornwall until the <a href="/wiki/1993_English_cricket_season" title="1993 English cricket season">1993 season</a>, by which time he had made eleven <a href="/wiki/Minor_Counties_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="Minor Counties Championship">Minor Counties Championship</a> appearances<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and three MCCA Knockout Trophy appearances.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h2><span
