id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sam_Llewellyn&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>Sam Llewellyn was born on <a href="/wiki/Tresco,_Isles_of_Scilly" title="Tresco, Isles of Scilly">Tresco</a>, <a href="/wiki/Isles_of_Scilly" title="Isles of Scilly">Isles of Scilly</a>, where his ancestors lived for many years.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He grew up in <a href="/wiki/Norfolk" title="Norfolk">Norfolk</a>. He attended <a href="/wiki/Eton_College" title="Eton College">Eton College</a> and later <a href="/wiki/St_Catherine%27s_College,_Oxford" title="St Catherine&#39;s College, Oxford">St Catherine\'s College, Oxford</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Llewellyn went on to live in <a href="/wiki/Toronto" title="Toronto">Toronto, Ontario</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>, before settling in <a href="/wiki/Herefordshire" title="Herefordshire">Herefordshire</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>, where he still lives.<sup id="cite_ref-Biography_of_Llewellyn_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Biography_of_Llewellyn-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>  Llewellyn has a deep love for the sea, and this has influenced much of his writing. He goes sailing regularly.\n</p><p>He was originally an editor and fine art dealer<sup id="cite_ref-fantasticfiction.co.uk_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fantasticfiction.co.uk-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> before becoming an author. He has become a prolific writer, and has written for both children and adults. He has also worked as a journalist with newspapers and magazines from both <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">America</a> and <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">Britain</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fantasticfiction.co.uk_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fantasticfiction.co.uk-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sam_Llewellyn&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>Llewellyn is married to <a href="/wiki/Canadians" title="Canadians">Canadian</a> children\'s author Karen Wallace.
