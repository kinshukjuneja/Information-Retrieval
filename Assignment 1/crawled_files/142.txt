/>.uk</a></span></td></tr></tbody></table>\n<p><b>North Dorset</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Non-metropolitan_district" title="Non-metropolitan district">local government district</a> in <a href="/wiki/Dorset" title="Dorset">Dorset</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>.  It is largely <a href="/wiki/Rural" class="mw-redirect" title="Rural">rural</a>, but includes the towns of <a href="/wiki/Blandford_Forum" title="Blandford Forum">Blandford Forum</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gillingham,_Dorset" title="Gillingham, Dorset">Gillingham</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shaftesbury" title="Shaftesbury">Shaftesbury</a>, <a href="/wiki/Stalbridge" title="Stalbridge">Stalbridge</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sturminster_Newton" title="Sturminster Newton">Sturminster Newton</a>.  Much of North Dorset is in the <a href="/wiki/River_Stour,_Dorset" title="River Stour, Dorset">River Stour</a> <a href="/wiki/Valley" title="Valley">valley</a> and is called the <a href="/wiki/Blackmore_Vale" title="Blackmore Vale">Blackmore Vale</a>.  The <a href="/wiki/Economics" title="Economics">economy</a> of North Dorset is largely <a href="/wiki/Dairy" title="Dairy">dairy</a> <a href="/wiki/Agriculture" title="Agriculture">agriculture</a> based.\n</p><p>The district was formed on 1 April 1974, under the <a href="/wiki/Local_Government_Act_1972" title="Local Government Act 1972">Local Government Act 1972</a>, from the <a href="/wiki/Municipal_borough" title="Municipal borough">municipal boroughs</a> of Blandford Forum, Shaftesbury, <a href="/w/index.php?title=Blandford_Rural_District&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Blandford Rural District (page does not exist)">Blandford Rural District</a>, <a href="/w/index.php?title=Shaftesbury_Rural_District&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Shaftesbury Rural District (page does not exist)">Shaftesbury Rural District</a> and <a href="/w/index.php?title=Sturminster_Rural_District&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Sturminster Rural District (page does not exist)">Sturminster Rural District</a>. The district and its council will be abolished on 1st April 2019 and, together with the other 4 districts outside the greater Bournemouth area, will form a new <a href="/wiki/Dorset_(unitary_authority)" title="Dorset (unitary authority)">Dorset</a> unitary authority.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>At the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom_Census_2001" class="mw-redirect" title="United Kingdom Census 2001">2001 UK census</a> North Dorset had a <a href="/wiki/Population" title="Population">population</a> of 61,905, a rise of 8,300 from 1991, with 25,248 households.\n</p><p>North Dorset is home to <a href="/wiki/North_Dorset_Rugby_Football_Club" title="North Dorset Rugby Football Club">North Dorset
