Then</b></i> is a community magazine distributed throughout the <a href="/wiki/Isles_of_Scilly" title="Isles of Scilly">Isles of Scilly</a>. It was launched in the spring of 2006.\n</p>\n<div id="toc" class="toc"><input type="checkbox" role="button" id="toctogglecheckbox" class="toctogglecheckbox" style="display:none" /><div class="toctitle" lang="en" dir="ltr"><h2>Contents</h2><span class="toctogglespan"><label class="toctogglelabel" for="toctogglecheckbox"></label></span></div>\n<ul>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-1"><a href="#Content"><span class="tocnumber">1</span> <span class="toctext">Content</span></a></li>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-2"><a href="#Distribution"><span class="tocnumber">2</span> <span class="toctext">Distribution</span></a></li>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-3"><a href="#Funding"><span class="tocnumber">3</span> <span class="toctext">Funding</span></a></li>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-4"><a href="#References"><span class="tocnumber">4</span> <span class="toctext">References</span></a></li>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-5"><a href="#External_links"><span class="tocnumber">5</span> <span class="toctext">External links</span></a></li>\n</ul>\n</div>\n\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Content">Content</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Scilly_Now_%26_Then&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Content">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The magazine is aimed at both locals and visitors. Local interest contributions are submitted by numerous public bodies including the police, <a href="/wiki/Harbourmaster" title="Harbourmaster">harbourmaster</a>, <a href="/wiki/Five_Islands_School" title="Five Islands School">Five Islands School</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fire_service" class="mw-redirect" title="Fire service">fire service</a>, <a href="/wiki/Health_centre" class="mw-redirect" title="Health centre">health centre</a>, local <a href="/wiki/AONB" class="mw-redirect" title="AONB">AONB</a> and Member of Parliament, <a href="/wiki/Derek_Thomas_(politician)" title="Derek Thomas (politician)">Derek Thomas</a>. There are also contributions from the local <a href="/wiki/RSPB" class="mw-redirect" title="RSPB">RSPB</a> officer and Chairman of the  <a href="/wiki/Council_of_the_Isles_of_Scilly" title="Council of the Isles of Scilly">Council of the Isles of Scilly</a>. Historical content includes regular features on local shipwrecks from diver Todd Stevens, maritime history from <a href="/wiki/Richard_Larn" title="Richard Larn">Richard Larn</a> <a href="/wiki/OBE" class="mw-redirect" title="OBE">OBE</a> and Professor <a href="/wiki/Wyn_Grant" title="Wyn Grant">Wyn Grant</a> from the University of Warwick provides information from <a href="/wiki/The_National_Archives" class="mw-redirect" title="The National Archives">The National Archives</a> in <a href="/wiki/Kew" title="Kew">Kew</a> and wildlife expert <a href="/wiki/Will_Wagstaff" title="Will Wagstaff">Will Wagstaff</a> provides information on the variety of birds on the islands. Editorial contributions from both the local community<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and visitors<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> are encouraged and there are regular items including a recipe, details on the night sky, a quiz and poetry submissions.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> There is also a letters page. A key function of the publication is to support the islands as an outstanding visitor destination so when controversial subjects are raised the editorial policy is to seek both sides of the argument.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Scilly Now &amp; Then has featured several interviews with celebrities including <a href="/wiki/Bill_Bryson" title="Bill Bryson">Bill Bryson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Brian_Cox_(physicist)" title="Brian Cox (physicist)">Brian Cox</a>, <a href="/wiki/Susie_Dent" title="Susie Dent">Susie Dent</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bill_Oddie" title="Bill Oddie">Bill Oddie</a>.\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Distribution">Distribution</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Scilly_Now_%26_Then&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Distribution">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>Distribution varies owing to the seasonal aspect of life on the islands with around 2000 copies in the winter months and up to 5,000 in peak season. Releases are titled Spring, April, May, June, July, August, Autumn and Winter and copies are supplied to a variety of outlets throughout the islands including the <a href="/wiki/Tourist_Information_Centre" class="mw-redirect" title="Tourist Information Centre">Tourist Information Centre</a>, shops, library, museum and airport.\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Funding">Funding</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Scilly_Now_%26_Then&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Funding">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>Scilly Now &amp; Then relies almost entirely on local advertising although some extra revenue is generated from visitors
