<b>Southern League</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Minor_League_Baseball" title="Minor League Baseball">Minor League Baseball</a> league that operates in the <a href="/wiki/Southern_United_States" title="Southern United States">Southern United States</a>. Classified a <a href="/wiki/Double-A_(baseball)" title="Double-A (baseball)">Double-A</a> league, it is headquartered in the <a href="/wiki/Atlanta" title="Atlanta">Atlanta</a> suburb of <a href="/wiki/Marietta,_Georgia" title="Marietta, Georgia">Marietta</a>. id="Origins">Origins</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Southern_League_(baseball)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Origins">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The <a href="/wiki/Southern_League_(1885%E2%80%9399)" title="Southern League (1885\xe2\x80\x9399)">original Southern League</a> existed from 1885 to 1899. A new league, the <a href="/wiki/Southern_Association" title="Southern Association">Southern Association</a>, was formed in <a href="/wiki/1901_in_baseball" title="1901 in baseball">1901</a>, consisting of twelve teams. The  Southern Association shut down after the <a href="/wiki/1961_in_baseball" title="1961 in baseball">1961</a> season.\n</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/1964_in_baseball" title="1964 in baseball">1964</a>, the original South Atlantic League (nicknamed the <i>Sally League</i> and not related to the <a href="/wiki/South_Atlantic_League" title="South Atlantic League">current league of the same name, formerly the <i>Western Carolinas League</i></a>) changed its name to the <i>Southern League</i>. The original South Atlantic League was a mid- to higher-classification circuit that played from 1904 to 1917, 1919 to 1930 (when it was known as the <i>South Atlantic Association</i>), 1936 to 1942, and 1946 to 1963. The old South Atlantic League grew from Class C status to Class B (through 1942) and then Class A (until 1962). In its final season, it was elevated to Double-A.\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Makeup">Makeup</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Southern_League_(baseball)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Makeup">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The Southern League is divided into two divisions, <i>North</i> and <i>South</i>. \n</p><p>The current league president is Lori Webb; the position was held by Steve DeSalvo as interim President for a few months due to the March 2012 death of former major league first baseman <a
