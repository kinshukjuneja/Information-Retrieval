(370&#160;m)</td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Length</th><td>\n11 miles (17&#160;km)</td></tr></tbody></table>\n<p>The <b>River Seaton</b> is a river in east <a href="/wiki/Cornwall" title="Cornwall">Cornwall</a>, England, UK which flows southwards for 11 miles (17&#160;km) into the <a href="/wiki/English_Channel" title="English Channel">English Channel</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:CRT:_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:CRT:-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>The river rises near <a href="/wiki/Caradon_Hill" title="Caradon Hill">Caradon Hill</a> and flows generally south past <a href="/wiki/Darite" title="Darite">Darite</a>, <a href="/wiki/Menheniot" title="Menheniot">Menheniot</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hessenford" title="Hessenford">Hessenford</a> and a few miles farther south into the sea at Seaton Beach, west of <a href="/wiki/Downderry" title="Downderry">Downderry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The river has been dug into channels in its lower reaches which has sped the flow of the water up and straightened the curves out. Additionally, because of the mining history around Bodmin Moor, the water is polluted with traces of aluminium and copper; both these effects have led to smaller populations of wildlife inhabiting the river.<sup id="cite_ref-:CRT:_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:CRT:-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>There is an early record of the river as "Seythyn" in 1302; the name means "little arrow river".<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="See_also">See also</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=River_Seaton&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: See also">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Seaton_Valley_Countryside_Park" title="Seaton Valley Countryside Park">Seaton Valley Countryside Park</a></li></ul>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="References">References</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=River_Seaton&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: References">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<div class="reflist" style="list-style-type: decimal;">\n<div class="mw-references-wrap"><ol class="references">\n<li id="cite_note-:CRT:-1"><span class="mw-cite-backlink">^ <a href="#cite_ref-:CRT:_1-0"><sup><i><b>a</b></i></sup></a> <a href="#cite_ref-:CRT:_1-1"><sup><i><b>b</b></i></sup></a></span> <span class="reference-text"><cite class="citation web"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.cornwallriversproject.org.uk/geography/seaton.htm">"Cornwall Rivers Project | Geography | Seaton"</a>. <i>www.cornwallriversproject.org.uk</i><span class="reference-accessdate">.
