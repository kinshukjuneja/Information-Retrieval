class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Why_the_Whales_Came&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot summary">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>\'Why the Whales Came\' is about ten-year-old Gracie Jenkins, who lives on Bryher, a small island off the western coast of Britain, in the year 1914. "You keep away from the Birdman," Gracie\'s father warns her. The Birdman lives alone in a cottage that stands all by itself on a hill in the south part of the island. Gracie\'s father knows stories about him that he thinks are too horrible to tell her. The Birdman used to live on Samson Island, which people say has a curse on it.  \n</p><p>Gracie and her friend Daniel have a fleet of toy boats they have made. When the lake where they usually sail the boats is taken over by bad-tempered swans, Daniel talks Gracie into coming with him to a cove near the Birdman\'s cottage. She\'s scared, but she finally agrees. Soon they find themselves on the most frightening adventure of their lives.\n</p><p>The whales in the novel are narwhals, a type of whale with a long, spiraling horn on the front of its head. In their adventure, Gracie and Daniel find a narwhal\'s horn. Later, they have to decide whether to help a stranded narwhal. They then rescue the narwhal. Later, the Birdman comes back to Bryher, and he is welcomed back by everybody.\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Characters">Characters</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Why_the_Whales_Came&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Characters">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<ul><li>Gracie Jenkins \xe2\x80\x94At the beginning, Gracie was a habitually fearful girl that believed any story told to her about the Birdman. She also believed in ghosts and the curses on Samson Island. Her best friend is Daniel Pender.</li>\n<li>Daniel Pender\xe2\x80\x94Daniel is a handy boy, but he is also adventurous and social. But Daniel is also afraid of the dark.</li>\n<li>Zachariah Woodcock\xe2\x80\x94A deaf old man that lives in a cottage away from all the island people also known as the Birdman. He is also a wonderful wood worker that makes bird sculptures. Woodcock likes traveling by sea to Samson with his dog Prince.</li>\n<li>Peter Jenkins\xe2\x80\x94The father of Gracie, he has a slightly angry personality in the story, but is also a good liar/actor at the same time.</li>\n<li>Clemmie Jenkins\xe2\x80\x94The mother of Gracie who possesses a calm but "weird" personality. Not so much an actor in the story, but she is really fearful.</li>\n<li>Treve Pender\xe2\x80\x94The father of Daniel. Not mentioned a lot in the story.</li>\n<li>Mary Pender\xe2\x80\x94The mother of Daniel. Not mentioned a lot in the story.</li>\n<li>Mr. Wellbeloved\xe2\x80\x94Gracie and Daniel\'s teacher who judges every student by arithmetic. He also predicted that the war will come. That came true later in the story.</li>\n<li>Big Tim Pender\xe2\x80\x94Tim is a bully to Gracie and Daniel, but he is also Daniel\'s brother.</li></ul>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline"
