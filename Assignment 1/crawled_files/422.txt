href="/wiki/Isles_of_Scilly" title="Isles of Scilly">Isles of Scilly</a>.  The football league is the smallest in the world,<sup id="cite_ref-WSL_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WSL-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Guardian_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Guardian-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> with only two clubs.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>The league is affiliated with <a href="/wiki/The_Football_Association" title="The Football Association">The Football Association</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WSL_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WSL-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<div id="toc" class="toc"><input type="checkbox" role="button" id="toctogglecheckbox" class="toctogglecheckbox" style="display:none" /><div class="tocnumber">6</span> <span class="toctext">External links</span></a></li>\n</ul>\n</div>\n\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isles_of_Scilly_Football_League&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>In the 1920s, the <b>Lyonnesse Inter-Island Cup</b>, a competition between the islands of <a href="/wiki/St._Mary%27s,_Isles_of_Scilly" class="mw-redirect" title="St. Mary&#39;s, Isles of Scilly">St. Mary\'s</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tresco,_Isles_of_Scilly" title="Tresco, Isles of Scilly">Tresco</a>, <a href="/wiki/St_Martin%27s,_Isles_of_Scilly" title="St Martin&#39;s, Isles of Scilly">St. Martins</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bryher,_Isles_of_Scilly" class="mw-redirect" title="Bryher, Isles of Scilly">Bryher</a> and <a href="/wiki/St._Agnes,_Isles_of_Scilly" class="mw-redirect" title="St. Agnes, Isles of Scilly">St. Agnes</a>, was formed.  By the 1950s, only two clubs remained - the <b>Rangers</b> and the <b>Rovers</b>.  In 1984 the two clubs changed their names to the <b>Garrison Gunners</b> and the <b>Woolpack Wanderers</b>, which are their current names.<sup id="cite_ref-WSL_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WSL-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Guardian_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Guardian-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>The League is attempting to get into the <a href="/wiki/Guinness_World_Records" title="Guinness World Records">Guinness World Records</a> for the smallest league in the world.<sup id="cite_ref-WSL_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WSL-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>In April 2008, <a href="/wiki/Adidas" title="Adidas">Adidas</a> ran an advertisement called "Dream Big", highlighting the league, featuring several well-known football personnel including <a href="/wiki/David_Beckham" title="David Beckham">David Beckham</a>, <a href="/wiki/Steven_Gerrard" title="Steven Gerrard">Steven Gerrard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Patrick_Vieira" title="Patrick Vieira">Patrick Vieira</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-adidas_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-adidas-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Competition_structure">Competition structure</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isles_of_Scilly_Football_League&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Competition structure">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Isles_of_Scilly_Football_League_game.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c5/Isles_of_Scilly_Football_League_game.jpg/220px-Isles_of_Scilly_Football_League_game.jpg" width="220" height="165" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c5/Isles_of_Scilly_Football_League_game.jpg/330px-Isles_of_Scilly_Football_League_game.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c5/Isles_of_Scilly_Football_League_game.jpg/440px-Isles_of_Scilly_Football_League_game.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2592" data-file-height="1944" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Isles_of_Scilly_Football_League_game.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Game in progress between the two teams - the Garrison Gunners in yellow, and the Woolpack Wanderers in red.</div></div></div>\n<p>The League competition involves the Woolpack Wanderers and the Garrison Gunners playing each other seventeen times, often on a Sunday.  There also exist two Cups: The Wholesalers Cup and the Foredeck Cup, which is played over two legs.  An "Old Men versus the Youngsters" match is played on <a href="/wiki/Boxing_Day" title="Boxing Day">Boxing Day</a>.  The season itself starts with the Charity Shield.<sup id="cite_ref-WSL_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WSL-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>  All the matches are played on the Garrison football field, on the island of <a href="/wiki/St._Mary%27s,_Isles_of_Scilly" class="mw-redirect" title="St. Mary&#39;s, Isles of Scilly">St. Mary\'s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-guardian_2006_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-guardian_2006-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>The league is played during the winter, from mid-November until the end of March.<sup id="cite_ref-WSL_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WSL-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-fixtures_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fixtures-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>Occasionally, a combined Scilly Isles team play a team <a href="/w/index.php?title=Newlyn_Non_Athletico&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Newlyn Non Athletico (page does not exist)">Newlyn Non Athletico</a>, a team at level 14 of the <a href="/wiki/English_football_league_system" title="English football league system">English football league system</a>.  A team from <a href="/wiki/Truro" title="Truro">Truro</a> visits annually to play against a combined team.<sup id="cite_ref-guardian_2006_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-guardian_2006-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Honours">Honours</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isles_of_Scilly_Football_League&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Honours">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The Woolpack Wanderers have won the Charity Cup 5 times - in 2002, 2004, 2005, 2006 and 2007.  In addition, they have won the Lioness Shield twice - in 2006 and 2007.<sup id="cite_ref-trophies_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-trophies-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>The Garrison Gunners have won the Lioness Shield once, in 2005.<sup id="cite_ref-trophies_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-trophies-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Concerns">Concerns</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isles_of_Scilly_Football_League&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Concerns">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The Isles of Scilly are struggling to hold on to their young people.  There is no sixth form on the Isles so when youngsters turn 16, they go to the mainland.  In addition, house prices are expensive, so they tend not to return until much older.  As a result, the number of players in the league is dwindling.  Howard Cole, a secretary who referees the games, estimated the average age of both teams to be within the mid- to late-30s.<sup id="cite_ref-guardian_2006_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-guardian_2006-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="References">References</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isles_of_Scilly_Football_League&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: References">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<div class="reflist" style="list-style-type: decimal;">\n<div class="mw-references-wrap"><ol class="references">\n<li id="cite_note-WSL-1"><span class="mw-cite-backlink">^ <a href="#cite_ref-WSL_1-0"><sup><i><b>a</b></i></sup></a> <a href="#cite_ref-WSL_1-1"><sup><i><b>b</b></i></sup></a> <a href="#cite_ref-WSL_1-2"><sup><i><b>c</b></i></sup></a> <a href="#cite_ref-WSL_1-3"><sup><i><b>d</b></i></sup></a> <a href="#cite_ref-WSL_1-4"><sup><i><b>e</b></i></sup></a> <a href="#cite_ref-WSL_1-5"><sup><i><b>f</b></i></sup></a></span> <span class="reference-text"><cite class="citation web"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://web.archive.org/web/20080928043310/http://www.worldssmallestleague.co.uk/html/about_us.html">"WSL About Us"</a>. worldssmallestleague.co.uk.
