href="/wiki/Battle_for_Henderson_Field" title="Battle for Henderson Field">Battle for Henderson Field</a></b> (23\xe2\x80\x9326 October 1942) on <a href="/wiki/Guadalcanal" title="Guadalcanal">Guadalcanal</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Solomon_Islands" title="Solomon Islands">Solomon Islands</a> was the third of three land offensives conducted by the <a href="/wiki/Empire_of_Japan" title="Empire of Japan">Japanese</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Guadalcanal_campaign" class="mw-redirect" title="Guadalcanal campaign">Guadalcanal campaign</a> of <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">World War II</a>. <a href="/wiki/United_States_Army" title="United States Army">U.S. Army</a> and <a href="/wiki/United_States_Marine_Corps" title="United States Marine Corps">Marine</a> forces under <a href="/wiki/Major_general_(United_States)" title="Major general (United States)">Major General</a> <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Vandegrift" title="Alexander Vandegrift">Alexander Vandegrift</a> defended <a href="/wiki/Honiara_International_Airport" title="Honiara International Airport">Henderson Field</a> against attacks by the <a href="/wiki/Seventeenth_Army_(Japan)" title="Seventeenth Army (Japan)">Japanese 17th Army</a> under <a href="/wiki/Lieutenant_General" class="mw-redirect" title="Lieutenant General">Lieutenant General</a> <a href="/wiki/Harukichi_Hyakutake" title="Harukichi Hyakutake">Harukichi Hyakutake</a> <i>(pictured)</i>. Hyakutake\'s mission was to recapture the airfield from the <a href="/wiki/Allies_of_World_War_II" title="Allies of World War II">Allies</a> and drive them off the island. Numerous assaults over three days were repulsed with heavy Japanese losses, and Allied aircraft operating from the airfield successfully defended U.S. positions from attacks by <a href="/wiki/Imperial_Japanese_Army" title="Imperial Japanese Army">Japanese naval</a> air and sea forces. After a  <a href="/wiki/Naval_Battle_of_Guadalcanal" title="Naval Battle of Guadalcanal">failed attempt to deliver reinforcements</a> in November, Japan conceded the island and <a href="/wiki/Operation_Ke" title="Operation Ke">successfully evacuated</a> many of its remaining forces in February. <b><a href="/wiki/B%C3%A9zier_curve" title="B\xc3\xa9zier curve">B\xc3\xa9zier curve</a></b> is a <a href="/wiki/Parametric_equation" title="Parametric equation">parametric curve</a> used in <a Other uses include the design of computer <a href="/wiki/Font" title="Font">fonts</a> and animation. B\xc3\xa9zier curves can be combined to form a <a href="/wiki/Composite_B%C3%A9zier_curve" title="Composite B\xc3\xa9zier curve">B\xc3\xa9zier spline</a>, or generalized to higher dimensions to form <a href="/wiki/B%C3%A9zier_surface" title="B\xc3\xa9zier surface">B\xc3\xa9zier surfaces</a>.\n</p>\n<p><small>Image: <a href="/wiki/User_talk:Twirlip" title="User talk:Twirlip">Phil
