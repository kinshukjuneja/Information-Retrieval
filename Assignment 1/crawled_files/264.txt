It is a compact <a href="/wiki/Perennial_plant" title="Perennial plant">perennial</a> which grows in low clumps and sends up long stems that support globes of bright pink flowers.  In some cases purple, white or red flowers also occur.  It is a popular garden flower and has been distributed worldwide as a garden and cut flower.  It does well in gardens designed as <a href="/wiki/Xeriscaping" title="Xeriscaping">xeriscapes</a> or <a href="/wiki/Rock_garden" title="Rock garden">rock gardens</a>.\n</p>\n<div id="toc"
