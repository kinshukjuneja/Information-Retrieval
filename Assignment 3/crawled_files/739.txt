Yorkshire">Yorkshire</a></dd></dl>\n</td></tr></tbody></table>\n<p>The <b>West Yorkshire Built-up Area</b>, previously known as the <b>West Yorkshire Urban Area</b> is a term used by the <a href="/wiki/Office_for_National_Statistics" title="Office for National Statistics">Office for National Statistics</a> (ONS) to refer to a conurbation in <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>, based on the cities of <a href="/wiki/Leeds" title="Leeds">Leeds</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bradford" title="Bradford">Bradford</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wakefield" title="Wakefield">Wakefield</a>, and the large towns of <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a> and <a href="/wiki/Halifax,_West_Yorkshire" title="Halifax, West Yorkshire">Halifax</a>. It is the 4th largest urban area in the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>. However, it excludes towns such as <a href="/wiki/Castleford" title="Castleford">Castleford</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pontefract" title="Pontefract">Pontefract</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wetherby" title="Wetherby">Wetherby</a> which though part of the county of West Yorkshire are considered independently. There are substantial areas of agricultural land within the designated area - more than in any other official urban area in England - many of the towns and cities are only just connected with one another by narrow outlying strips of development.\n</p>\n<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:312px;"><a href="/wiki/File:WestYorkshireUrbanArea.svg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/72/WestYorkshireUrbanArea.svg/310px-WestYorkshireUrbanArea.svg.png" width="310" height="325" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/72/WestYorkshireUrbanArea.svg/465px-WestYorkshireUrbanArea.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/72/WestYorkshireUrbanArea.svg/620px-WestYorkshireUrbanArea.svg.png 2x" data-file-width="1176" data-file-height="1232" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:WestYorkshireUrbanArea.svg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>West Yorkshire Urban Area map in 2001</div></div></div>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Urban_subdivisions">Urban subdivisions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=West_Yorkshire_Urban_Area&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Urban subdivisions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The ONS gives the conurbation a population of 1,777,934 (2011 census),<sup id="cite_ref-BUA_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BUA-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> which makes it the <a href="/wiki/List_of_conurbations_in_the_United_Kingdom" class="mw-redirect" title="List of conurbations in the United Kingdom">fourth-most populous in the UK</a>.
