Kingdom">part of</a> the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-Stats_1_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Stats_1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
