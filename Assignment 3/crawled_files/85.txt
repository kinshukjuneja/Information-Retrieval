class="mw-indicators mw-body-content">\n</div>\n<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">David Hunt (ornithologist)</h1>\t\t\t<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">\n\t\t\t\t<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>\t\t\t\t<div id="contentSub"></div>\n\t\t\t\t<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>\t\t\t\t<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>\n\t\t\t\t<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>\n\t\t\t\t<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>David Bassil Hunt</b> (1934, <a href="/wiki/Devonport,_Plymouth" title="Devonport, Plymouth">Devonport, Plymouth</a><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> \xe2\x80\x93 22 February 1985) was an <a href="/wiki/English_people" title="English people">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Ornithologist" class="mw-redirect" title="Ornithologist">ornithologist</a> who worked on the <a href="/wiki/Isles_of_Scilly" title="Isles of Scilly">Isles of Scilly</a>, an <a href="/wiki/Archipelago" title="Archipelago">archipelago</a> off the south west coast of England. He was killed by a <a href="/wiki/Tiger" title="Tiger">tiger</a>, the first <a href="/wiki/European_diaspora" class="mw-redirect" title="European diaspora">European</a> to suffer this fate for many decades.<sup id="cite_ref-Flumm1985_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Flumm1985-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>Brought up on the <a href="/wiki/Yealm" class="mw-redirect" title="Yealm">Yealm</a> estuary, he attended <a href="/wiki/Gresham%27s_School" title="Gresham&#39;s School">Gresham\'s School</a> in Norfolk and, in 1954 studied art and went on to work as a professional jazz <a href="/wiki/Trombone" title="Trombone">trombonist</a> in Germany. On returning to Britain he worked as a "horticulturalist" which led to <a href="/wiki/Tresco,_Isles_of_Scilly" title="Tresco, Isles of Scilly">Tresco</a> on the Isles of Scilly, and the Island Hotel where he became the gardener in 1964. He was based on the Isles of Scilly for many years, working as an international <a href="/wiki/Birding" class="mw-redirect" title="Birding">birding</a> tour guide.\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Death">Death</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=David_Hunt_(ornithologist)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Death">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>He was killed by a <a href="/wiki/Bengal_tiger" title="Bengal tiger">Bengal tiger</a> in <a href="/wiki/Jim_Corbett_National_Park" title="Jim Corbett National Park">Jim Corbett National Park</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Nainital_district" title="Nainital district">Nainital district</a> of <a href="/wiki/Uttarakhand" title="Uttarakhand">Uttarakhand</a> in northern <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a>, while leading a bird tour there,<sup id="cite_ref-Flumm1985_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Flumm1985-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> the same year that his autobiography, <i>Confessions of a Scilly Birdman</i>, was published. As a result of this incident, visitors are now forbidden to walk on the territory of the Jim Corbett National Park any time of the day if they are unattended by a certified guide.\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="References">References</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=David_Hunt_(ornithologist)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: References">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<div class="reflist" style="list-style-type: decimal;">\n<div class="mw-references-wrap"><ol class="references">\n<li id="cite_note-1"><span class="mw-cite-backlink"><b><a href="#cite_ref-1">^</a></b></span> <span class="reference-text"><a rel="nofollow" class="external free" href="http://search.findmypast.co.uk/results/world-records/england-and-wales-births-1837-2006?firstname=david%20b&amp;lastname=hunt&amp;eventyear=1934&amp;eventyear_offset=0&amp;mothersmaidenname=bassil">http://search.findmypast.co.uk/results/world-records/england-and-wales-births-1837-2006?firstname=david%20b&amp;lastname=hunt&amp;eventyear=1934&amp;eventyear_offset=0&amp;mothersmaidenname=bassil</a></span>\n</li>\n<li id="cite_note-Flumm1985-2"><span class="mw-cite-backlink">^ <a href="#cite_ref-Flumm1985_2-0"><sup><i><b>a</b></i></sup></a> <a href="#cite_ref-Flumm1985_2-1"><sup><i><b>b</b></i></sup></a></span> <span class="reference-text">Flumm, D.
