href="http://www.cartography.org.uk">www<wbr />.cartography<wbr />.org<wbr />.uk</a></span></td></tr></tbody></table>\n<p>The <b>British Cartographic Society (BCS)</b> is an association of individuals and organisations dedicated to exploring and developing the world of maps. It is a <a href="/wiki/Charitable_organization#Charity_registration" title="Charitable organization">registered charity</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Membership includes mapping companies, publishers, designers, academics, researchers, map curators, individual cartographers, <a href="/wiki/GIS" class="mw-redirect" title="GIS">GIS</a> specialists and ordinary members of the public with an interest in <a href="/wiki/Map" title="Map">maps</a>.\n</p><p>The BCS is regarded as one of the world\'s leading cartographic societies and its main publication, <b>The Cartographic Journal</b>, is recognised internationally.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Membership of the Society can be useful for making contacts and keeping up with developments.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>The BCS promotes all aspects of <a href="/wiki/Cartography" title="Cartography">cartography</a> to a wide range of potential users.\n</p>\n<div of the Society is to encourage better mapping by providing medals and prizes to individuals or companies who have shown excellence in this field. an introduction</i> 2nd Edition - a practical guide to making better maps - New, enlarged and expanded edition including thematic cartography, web mapping and coordinate systems</li>\n<li><i>A Celebration of 50 years of the British Cartographic Society</i>, published by the Society to mark its 50th anniversary</li>\n<li><i>Cartography -
