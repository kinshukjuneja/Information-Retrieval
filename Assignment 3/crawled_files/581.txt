class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Demographics_of_Saint_Helena,_Ascension_and_Tristan_da_Cunha&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Nationality">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">See also: <a href="/wiki/British_nationality_law" title="British nationality law">British nationality law</a></div>\n<p>The citizens of <a href="/wiki/Saint_Helena,_Ascension_and_Tristan_da_Cunha" title="Saint Helena, Ascension and Tristan da Cunha">Saint Helena, Ascension and Tristan da Cunha</a> hold <a href="/wiki/British_Overseas_Territories_citizen" title="British Overseas Territories citizen">British Overseas Territories citizenship</a>. On 21 May 2002 they were granted access to full British citizenship by the <a href="/wiki/British_Overseas_Territories_Act_2002" title="British Overseas Territories Act 2002">British Overseas Territories Act 2002</a> and there is a special <a href="/wiki/Saint_Helena_passport" class="mw-redirect" title="Saint Helena passport">Saint Helena passport</a> issued to them.\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Saint_Helena">Saint Helena</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Demographics_of_Saint_Helena,_Ascension_and_Tristan_da_Cunha&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Saint Helena">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p><a href="/wiki/Saint_Helena" title="Saint Helena">Saint Helena</a> is the most populous part of <a href="/wiki/Saint_Helena,_Ascension_and_Tristan_da_Cunha" title="Saint Helena, Ascension and Tristan da Cunha">Saint Helena, Ascension and Tristan da Cunha</a>. The language spoken in Saint Helena is English.\n</p><p>The island has a small population of a few thousand inhabitants, mainly descended from <a href="/wiki/Africans" class="mw-redirect" title="Africans">Africans</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mixed_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Mixed race">Mixed race</a> African and Europeans, British settlers, <a href="/wiki/East_India_Company" title="East India Company">East India Company</a> employees and indentured labourers from the <a href="/wiki/South_Asian" class="mw-redirect" title="South Asian">South Asian</a> sub-Continent, <a href="/wiki/East_Indies" title="East Indies">East Indies</a>, <a href="/wiki/Madagascar" title="Madagascar">Madagascar</a> and China.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>The people of Saint Helena are <i>Saint Helenians</i> (though locally they are known as "Saints"); the <a href="/wiki/Demonym" title="Demonym">demonym</a> being <i>Saint Helenian</i>.\n</p><p>A <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a> in February 2016 recorded a population of 4,534 on the island.<sup id="cite_ref-census2016_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-census2016-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> This compares with a figure of 4,257 recorded in 2008<sup id="cite_ref-census2008_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-census2008-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and a figure of 5,157 recorded in 1998. The fall between 1998 and 2008 can be explained mostly by emigration, especially since 2002 when the islanders were granted full British citizenship. The main <a href="/wiki/Diaspora" title="Diaspora">diasporas</a> are to the United Kingdom, South Africa and more recently to the <a href="/wiki/Falkland_Islands" title="Falkland Islands">Falkland Islands</a>, as well as on <a href="/wiki/Ascension_Island" title="Ascension Island">Ascension Island</a>. The population density, based on the 2016 figure, is 37.3 persons per km<sup>2</sup>, or 95.3 per sq mile.\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Districts_of_Saint_Helena">Districts of Saint Helena</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Demographics_of_Saint_Helena,_Ascension_and_Tristan_da_Cunha&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Districts of Saint Helena">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">See also: <a href="/wiki/Category:Parishes_of_Saint_Helena" title="Category:Parishes of Saint Helena">Category:Parishes of Saint Helena</a></div>\n<p>The island of Saint Helena is administratively divided into eight districts,<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> each with a community centre. The districts also serve as statistical subdivisions. id="Ethnicity">Ethnicity</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Demographics_of_Saint_Helena,_Ascension_and_Tristan_da_Cunha&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Ethnicity">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>\n<p>According to the <a href="/wiki/CIA_World_Factbook" class="mw-redirect" title="CIA World Factbook">CIA World Factbook</a>, by ethnicity 50% of the population of St Helena is <a href="/wiki/Black_people" title="Black people">Black</a> or <a href="/wiki/Native_ethnic_groups_of_Africa" class="mw-redirect" title="Native ethnic groups of Africa">African</a>, 25% is <a href="/wiki/Overseas_Chinese" title="Overseas Chinese">Chinese</a>, and a further 25% is <a href="/wiki/White_British" title="White British">White British</a>. However, the possibility of clear-cut categorical divisions between ethnicities on present day St Helena, as reflected by these statistics, is disputed.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Many St Helenians today are <a href="/wiki/Mixed-race" class="mw-redirect" title="Mixed-race">mixed-race</a>.\n</p>\n<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Religion">Religion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Demographics_of_Saint_Helena,_Ascension_and_Tristan_da_Cunha&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Religion">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>\n<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">See also: <a href="/wiki/Category:Religion_in_Saint_Helena" title="Category:Religion in Saint Helena">Category:Religion in Saint Helena</a></div>\n<p>The majority of St. Helenians are <a href="/wiki/Anglican" class="mw-redirect" title="Anglican">Anglican</a>. Other religions in St. Helena include (<i>in alphabetic order</i>): the <a href="/wiki/Bah%C3%A1%27%C3%AD" class="mw-redirect" title="Bah\xc3\xa1&#39;\xc3\xad">Bah\xc3\xa1\'\xc3\xad</a> Faith,<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> the <a href="/wiki/Baptist" class="mw-redirect" title="Baptist">Baptist</a> church, <a href="/wiki/Buddhism" title="Buddhism">Buddhism</a>, <a href="/wiki/Roman_Catholicism" class="mw-redirect" title="Roman Catholicism">Roman Catholicism</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Seventh-day_Adventist_Church" title="Seventh-day Adventist Church">Seventh-day Adventism</a>.\n</p><p>According to the statistics in the 2014 Yearbook of <a href="/wiki/Jehovah%27s_Witnesses" title="Jehovah&#39;s Witnesses">Jehovah\'s Witnesses</a>, Saint Helena has the highest proportion of Jehovah\'s Witnesses of any country or territory in the world: one person in thirty-five, using an estimated population of 4,000 for St Helena and a count of 118 members.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>In Tristan da Cunha, Christianity is the main religion, with the largest denominations being Anglican and Roman Catholic.\n</p>\n<table class="wikitable">\n\n<tbody><tr>\n<th>Religious</th>\n<th>Resident population (St Helena)</th>\n<th>Percentage <sup href="/w/index.php?title=Demographics_of_Saint_Helena,_Ascension_and_Tristan_da_Cunha&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>\n<p>Where being <a href="/wiki/Literate" class="mw-redirect" title="Literate">literate</a> is defined as being of age 20 or over and able to read and write, 97% percent of the total population is literate, according to estimates from 1987. 97% of males and 98% of females of St. Helena are literate under this definition by the same estimates.\n</p>\n<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Health">Health</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Demographics_of_Saint_Helena,_Ascension_and_Tristan_da_Cunha&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Health">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>\n<p>By 2010 estimates, <a href="/wiki/Life_expectancy" title="Life expectancy">life expectancy</a> at birth for the total population is 78.6 years (for males it is 75.68; for females it is 81.67). The infant mortality rate is 16.98 deaths per 1,000 live births.\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Ascension_Island_and_Tristan_da_Cunha">Ascension Island and Tristan da Cunha</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Demographics_of_Saint_Helena,_Ascension_and_Tristan_da_Cunha&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Ascension Island and Tristan da Cunha">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p><a href="/wiki/Ascension_Island" title="Ascension Island">Ascension Island</a> has no native inhabitants officially. A transient population of approximately 1,000 live on the Island, made up mainly of members of the <a href="/wiki/Military_of_the_United_States" class="mw-redirect" title="Military of the United States">American</a> and <a href="/wiki/Military_of_the_United_Kingdom" class="mw-redirect" title="Military of the United Kingdom">British militaries</a>, supporting civilian contractors who serve on the joint Anglo-American airbase, and members of their families (a few of whom were born on the island).\n</p><p><a href="/wiki/Tristan_da_Cunha" title="Tristan da Cunha">Tristan da Cunha</a> has a population of about three hundred inhabitants of mainly mixed-British, -African, and -Italian descent.\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="See_also">See also</span><span
