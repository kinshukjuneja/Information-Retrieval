Kingdom</h1>\t\t\t<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">\n\t\t\t\t<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>\t\t\t\t<div id="contentSub"></div>\n\t\t\t\t<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>\t\t\t\t<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>\n\t\t\t\t<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>\n\t\t\t\t<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>military history of the United Kingdom</b> covers the period from the creation of the united <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Great_Britain" title="Kingdom of Great Britain">Kingdom of Great Britain</a>, with the political union of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Scotland" title="Kingdom of Scotland">Scotland</a> in 1707,<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> to the present day.\n</p><p>From the 18th century, with the expansion of the <a href="/wiki/British_Empire" title="British Empire">British Empire</a> and the country\'s industrial strength, Britain\'s military force became one of the largest and most powerful in the world, particularly that of its navy, with advanced technology and bases across the world. It declined during the 20th century in the wake of two world wars, <a href="/wiki/Decolonisation" class="mw-redirect" title="Decolonisation">decolonisation</a>, and the rise of the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> and the <a href="/wiki/USSR" class="mw-redirect" title="USSR">USSR</a> as the new <a href="/wiki/Superpower" title="Superpower">superpowers</a>. Britain has been <a href="/wiki/List_of_wars_involving_Great_Britain" class="mw-redirect" title="List of wars involving Great Britain">involved in a great many armed conflicts</a> since the union in 1707, on all continents except for Antarctica. Today it still remains a <a href="/wiki/Major_power" class="mw-redirect" title="Major power">major power</a> with frequent military interventions across the globe since the end of the <a href="/wiki/Cold_War" title="Cold War">Cold War</a> in 1991.\n</p><p>The present-day <a href="/wiki/British_Armed_Forces" title="British Armed Forces">British Armed Forces</a> encompass the <a href="/wiki/Royal_Navy" title="Royal Navy">Royal Navy</a>, the <a href="/wiki/British_Army" title="British Army">British Army</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Royal_Air_Force" title="Royal Air Force">Royal Air Force</a>.\n</p>\n<div id="toc" class="toc"><input type="checkbox" role="button" id="toctogglecheckbox" class="toctogglecheckbox" style="display:none" /><div class="toctitle" lang="en"
