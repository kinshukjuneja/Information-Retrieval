class="mw-parser-output"><p>\n\nThe <b>Fresh Talent Initiative</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Government" title="Scottish Government">Scottish Government</a> policy framework to encourage people to settle in <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The initiative was launched in February 2004 by then <a href="/wiki/First_Minister_of_Scotland" title="First Minister of Scotland">First Minister</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jack_McConnell" title="Jack McConnell">Jack McConnell</a> as a way of countering the \'biggest challenge facing Scotland\' of its falling population.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> \n</p><p>Since immigration is a matter reserved to the UK parliament in the legislation that established the <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Parliament" title="Scottish Parliament">Scottish Parliament</a> in 1999 (the Scotland Act 1998), agreement had to be obtained from the <a href="/wiki/Home_Office" title="Home Office">Home Office</a> for a key part of the initiative: to allow overseas graduates from <a href="/wiki/Scottish_universities" class="mw-redirect" title="Scottish universities">Scottish universities</a>, who express the intention of living and working in Scotland, to stay on for two years following graduation to seek employment. The Fresh Talent: Working in Scotland Scheme was up and running by summer 2005, applying to those who graduated that year.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>Opposition towards the scheme was voiced by some <a href="/wiki/English_universities" class="mw-redirect" title="English universities">English universities</a> which felt that the scheme gave Scottish universities a competitive advantage in terms of attracting students from overseas.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>The Fresh Talent: Working in Scotland Scheme was subsumed into the UK immigration system on 29 June 2008 when the UK government brought in a new <a href="/wiki/Points-based_immigration_system_(United_Kingdom)" title="Points-based immigration system (United Kingdom)">points based immigration scheme</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a
