href="/wiki/Department_of_Education_(Northern_Ireland)" title="Department of Education (Northern Ireland)">Department of Education</a> in <a href="/wiki/Northern_Ireland" title="Northern Ireland">Northern Ireland</a>. It was established under the Education Act (Northern Ireland) 2014 (c. 12)<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> which was passed by the <a href="/wiki/Northern_Ireland_Assembly" title="Northern Ireland Assembly">Northern Ireland Assembly</a>. The authority became operational on 1 April 2015.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><div id="toc" class="toc"><input type="checkbox" role="button" id="toctogglecheckbox" class="toctogglecheckbox" style="display:none" /><div class="toctitle" lang="en" dir="ltr"><h2>Contents</h2><span class="toctogglespan"><label class="toctogglelabel" for="toctogglecheckbox"></label></span></div>\n<ul>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-1"><a href="#Responsibilities"><span class="tocnumber">1</span> <span class="toctext">Responsibilities</span></a></li>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-2"><a href="#Education_Authority_Board"><span class="tocnumber">2</span> <span class="toctext">Education Authority Board</span></a></li>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-3"><a href="#References"><span class="tocnumber">3</span> <span class="toctext">References</span></a></li>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-4"><a href="#External_links"><span class="tocnumber">4</span> <span class="toctext">External links</span></a></li>\n</ul>\n</div>\n\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Responsibilities">Responsibilities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Education_Authority&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Responsibilities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The Education Authority is responsible for ensuring that efficient and effective primary and secondary education services are available to meet the needs of children and young people, and support for the provision of efficient and effective youth services.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> These services were previously delivered by the five <a href="/wiki/Education_in_Northern_Ireland" title="Education in Northern Ireland">Education and Library Boards</a> (ELBs). Each of the former ELBs is now a sub region of the Education Authority:\n</p>\n<ul><li>Belfast Region<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>\n<li>North Eastern Region<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>\n<li>South Eastern Region<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></li>\n<li>Southern Region<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li>\n<li>Western Region<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Education_Authority_Board">Education Authority Board</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Education_Authority&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Education Authority Board">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The Education Authority Board consists of 20 members plus the Chair. These include:\n</p>\n<ul><li>8 political members who were nominated by political parties according to the <a href="/wiki/D%27Hondt_method" title="D&#39;Hondt method">D\xe2\x80\x99Hondt</a> mechanism;</li>\n<li>4 members representative of the interests of the Transferors (the <a href="/wiki/Church_of_Ireland" title="Church of Ireland">Church of Ireland</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Methodist_Church_in_Ireland" title="Methodist Church in Ireland">Methodist Church in Ireland</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Presbyterian_Church_in_Ireland" title="Presbyterian Church in Ireland">Presbyterian Church in Ireland</a>) of controlled schools;</li>\n<li>4 members representative of the interests of the Trustees of maintained schools;</li>\n<li>1 member representative of the interests of <a href="/wiki/Integrated_education" title="Integrated education">Integrated schools</a>;</li>\n<li>1 member representative of the interests of Irish medium schools;</li>\n<li>1 member representative of the interests of Voluntary Grammar schools; and</li>\n<li>1 member representative of the interests of Controlled Grammar schools<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="References">References</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Education_Authority&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: References">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<div class="mw-references-wrap"><ol class="references">\n<li id="cite_note-1"><span class="mw-cite-backlink"><b><a href="#cite_ref-1">^</a></b></span> <span class="reference-text"><cite class="citation web"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.legislation.gov.uk/nia/2014/12">"Education Act (Northern Ireland) 2014"</a>. <i>www.legislation.gov.uk</i><span class="reference-accessdate">.
