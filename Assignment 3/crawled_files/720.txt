<a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a population,<sup id="cite_ref-Population_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Population-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> while its mainland territory occupies most of the southern two-thirds of the island of <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>. England is bordered by <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a> to the north, <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Wales</a> to the west and the <a href="/wiki/North_Sea" title="North Sea">North Sea</a>, <a href="/wiki/Irish_Sea" title="Irish Sea">Irish Sea</a>, <a href="/wiki/Celtic_Sea" title="Celtic Sea">Celtic Sea</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bristol_Channel" title="Bristol Channel">Bristol Channel</a> and <a href="/wiki/English_Channel" title="English Channel">English Channel</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Capital_(political)" class="mw-redirect" title="Capital (political)">capital</a> is <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, the largest metropolitan area in Great Britain, and the largest urban zone in the <a href="/wiki/European_Union" title="European Union">European Union</a> by many measures.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;note
