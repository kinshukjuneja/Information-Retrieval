the <a href="/wiki/Office_for_National_Statistics" title="Office for National Statistics">Office for National Statistics</a> (ONS) to refer to a conurbation in based around the city of <a href="/wiki/Bristol" title="Bristol">Bristol</a>, in <a href="/wiki/South_West_England" title="South West England">South West England</a>. It was formerly known as the Bristol Urban Area. It is the 7th largest urban area in <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> and 8th largest in the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>. It covers the contiguous built-up area around Bristol, including the <a href="/wiki/Kingswood,_South_Gloucestershire" title="Kingswood, South Gloucestershire">Kingswood</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mangotsfield" title="Mangotsfield">Mangotsfield</a>, <a href="/wiki/Stoke_Gifford" title="Stoke Gifford">Stoke Gifford</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bradley_Stoke" title="Bradley Stoke">Bradley Stoke</a>, <a href="/wiki/Patchway" title="Patchway">Patchway</a>, <a href="/wiki/Filton" title="Filton">Filton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Almondsbury" title="Almondsbury">Almondsbury</a>, <a href="/wiki/Frampton_Cotterell" title="Frampton Cotterell">Frampton Cotterell</a> and <a href="/wiki/Winterbourne,_Gloucestershire" title="Winterbourne, Gloucestershire">Winterbourne</a> areas within <a href="/wiki/South_Gloucestershire" title="South Gloucestershire">South Gloucestershire</a>; <a href="/wiki/Pill,_Somerset" title="Pill, Somerset">Pill</a> and <a href="/wiki/Easton-in-Gordano" class="mw-redirect" title="Easton-in-Gordano">Easton-in-Gordano</a> within <a href="/wiki/North_Somerset" title="North Somerset">North Somerset</a>; and part of <a href="/wiki/Whitchurch,_Bristol" title="Whitchurch, Bristol">Whitchurch</a> which falls within the administrative area of <a href="/wiki/Bath_and_North_East_Somerset" title="Bath and North East Somerset">Bath and North East
