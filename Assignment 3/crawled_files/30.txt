topical guide to Cornwall:\n<b><a href="/wiki/Cornwall" title="Cornwall">Cornwall</a></b> &#8211; <a href="/wiki/Ceremonial_counties_of_England" title="Ceremonial counties of England">ceremonial county</a> and <a href="/wiki/Unitary_authorities_of_England" title="Unitary authorities of England">unitary authority area</a> of <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> within the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>. Cornwall is a peninsula bordered to the north and west by the <a href="/wiki/Celtic_Sea" title="Celtic Sea">Celtic Sea</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> to the south by the <a href="/wiki/English_Channel" title="English Channel">English Channel</a>, and to the east by the county of <a href="/wiki/Devon" title="Devon">Devon</a>, over the <a href="/wiki/River_Tamar" title="River Tamar">River Tamar</a>. Cornwall is also a <a href="/wiki/Royal_duchy" class="mw-redirect" title="Royal duchy">royal duchy</a> of the United Kingdom.
