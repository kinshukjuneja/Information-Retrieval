Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>Rochester was born 18 February 1997 in <a href="/wiki/Mitcham,_London" title="Mitcham, London">Mitcham</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_London" title="South London">South London</a>. He attended <a href="/wiki/Harris_Academy_Merton" title="Harris Academy Merton">Harris Academy Merton</a>, where he was excluded several times in secondary school, and further attended <a href="/wiki/Sixth_form" title="Sixth form">sixth form</a> there.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Rochester eventually went to study Sports Development at the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Portsmouth" title="University of Portsmouth">University of Portsmouth</a> before dropping out the same year to focus on music.<sup id="cite_ref-TLU_6-0" class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>Rochester released his first song "I\'m On My Grind" in February 2017 to little notice.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He further released several other tracks in March 2017<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> and July 2017.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Whilst releasing music, Rochester was attempting to find employment after leaving university, eventually finding a job working at supermarket chain <a href="/wiki/Sainsbury%27s" title="Sainsbury&#39;s">Sainsbury\'s</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-TLU_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TLU-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>In September 2017, Rochester formally released "Barking" alongside a music video.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> The song entered the UK Singles Chart on 14 December 2017 at number 41, before peaking at number two on 19 January 2018.<sup id="cite_ref-UK_4-1"
