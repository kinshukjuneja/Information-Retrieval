href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> is an English <a href="/wiki/Composer" title="Composer">composer</a>, <a href="/wiki/Orchestrator" class="mw-redirect" title="Orchestrator">orchestrator</a>, <a href="/wiki/Conducting" title="Conducting">conductor</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Music_producer" class="mw-redirect" title="Music producer">music producer</a>. He has regularly written for video games, television and films, such as <i><a href="/wiki/Metal_Gear" title="Metal Gear">Metal Gear</a></i> Solid series, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Chronicles_of_Narnia:_The_Lion,_the_Witch_and_the_Wardrobe" title="The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe">The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/X-Men_Origins:_Wolverine" title="X-Men Origins: Wolverine">X-Men Origins: Wolverine</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Martian_(film)" title="The Martian (film)">The Martian</a></i>, and the <a href="/wiki/Shrek_(franchise)" title="Shrek (franchise)"><i>Shrek</i> franchise</a>.
