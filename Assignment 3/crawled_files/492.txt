title="Gibraltar">Gibraltar</a>. Gibraltar is used primarily as a training area, thanks to its good climate and rocky terrain, and as a stopover for aircraft and ships <i>en route</i> to and from deployments <a href="/wiki/East_of_Suez" title="East of Suez">East of Suez</a> or Africa.\n</p>\n<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a
