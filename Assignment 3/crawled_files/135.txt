/>\n</p>\n</td></tr></tbody></table></td></tr></tbody></table>\n<p><br />\nThe <b>2013 Council of the Isles of Scilly election</b> took place on 2 May 2013 as part of the 2013 local elections in the United Kingdom The council is a <a href="/wiki/Sui_generis" title="Sui generis">sui generis</a> <a href="/wiki/Unitary_authority" title="Unitary authority">unitary authority</a> within the <a href="/wiki/Ceremonial_county" class="mw-redirect" title="Ceremonial county">ceremonial county</a> of <a href="/wiki/Cornwall" title="Cornwall">Cornwall</a>.\n</p><p>The whole council of 21 members was up for election, with thirteen members elected in the <a href="/wiki/St_Mary%27s,_Isles_of_Scilly" title="St Mary&#39;s, Isles of Scilly">St Mary\'s</a> electoral division and another eight from the \'Off Islands\': being two each from <a href="/wiki/Bryher,_Isles_of_Scilly" class="mw-redirect" title="Bryher, Isles of Scilly">Bryher</a>, <a href="/wiki/St_Martin%27s,_Isles_of_Scilly" title="St Martin&#39;s, Isles of Scilly">St Martin\'s</a>, <a href="/wiki/St_Agnes,_Isles_of_Scilly" title="St Agnes, Isles of Scilly">St Agnes</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tresco,_Isles_of_Scilly" title="Tresco, Isles of Scilly">Tresco</a>. Since only one candidate stood for each of six \'Off Island\' seats they were elected unopposed. The <a href="/wiki/Green_Party_of_England_and_Wales" title="Green Party of England and Wales">Green Party of England and Wales</a> put up candidates - the first time any party had done so,<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> but both were unsuccessful.\n</p><p>As with other unitary and county elections in England, these local elections in the Isles of Scilly took place for the first time since 1993 without a <a href="/wiki/European_Parliament_elections" class="mw-redirect" title="European Parliament elections">European</a> or <a href="/wiki/House_of_Commons_of_the_United_Kingdom" title="House of Commons of the United Kingdom">Westminster</a> election on the same day.\n</p>\n<div id="toc" class="toc"><input type="checkbox" role="button" id="toctogglecheckbox" class="toctogglecheckbox" style="display:none" /><div class="toctitle" lang="en" dir="ltr"><h2>Contents</h2><span class="toctogglespan"><label class="toctogglelabel" for="toctogglecheckbox"></label></span></div>\n<ul>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-1"><a href="#Results"><span class="tocnumber">1</span> <span class="toctext">Results</span></a>\n<ul>\n<li class="toclevel-2 tocsection-2"><a href="#By_ward"><span class="tocnumber">1.1</span> <span class="toctext">By ward</span></a></li>\n</ul>\n</li>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-3"><a href="#References"><span class="tocnumber">2</span> <span class="toctext">References</span></a></li>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-4"><a href="#External_links"><span class="tocnumber">3</span> <span class="toctext">External links</span></a></li>\n</ul>\n</div>\n\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Results">Results</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Council_of_the_Isles_of_Scilly_election,_2013&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Results">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<table class="wikitable" style=";
