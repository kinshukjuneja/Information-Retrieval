href="/wiki/Military_budget" title="Military budget">Military expenditure</a> figures are presented in <a href="/wiki/United_States_dollar" title="United States dollar">United States dollars</a> based on either constant or current exchange rates.<sup id="cite_ref-SIPRI_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SIPRI-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> These results can vary greatly from one year to another based on fluctuations in the <a href="/wiki/Exchange_rate" title="Exchange rate">exchange rates</a> of each country\'s currency. Such fluctuations may change a country\'s ranking from one year to the next.\n</p><p>The first list is based on the <a href="/wiki/Stockholm_International_Peace_Research_Institute" title="Stockholm International Peace Research Institute">Stockholm International Peace Research Institute</a> (SIPRI) 2018 Fact Sheet, which includes a list of the world\'s top 15 military spenders in 2018, based on
