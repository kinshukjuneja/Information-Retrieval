any language</div>\n<p>This is a list of <b>best-selling <a href="/wiki/Fiction" title="Fiction">fiction</a> authors</b> to date, in any language. While finding precise sales numbers for any given author is near-impossible, the list is based on approximate numbers provided or repeated by reliable sources. "Best-selling" refers to the estimated number of copies sold of all fiction books written or co-written by an author. To keep the list manageable, only authors with estimated sales of at least 100 million are included. Authors of <a href="/wiki/Comic_book" title="Comic book">comic books</a> are not included unless they have been published in book format (for example, <a href="/wiki/Franco-Belgian_comics#Formats" title="Franco-Belgian comics">comic albums</a>, <a href="/wiki/Manga" title="Manga">manga</a> <a href="/wiki/Tank%C5%8Dbon" title="Tank\xc5\x8dbon">tank\xc5\x8dbon</a> volumes, <a href="/wiki/Trade_paperback_(comics)" title="Trade paperback (comics)">trade paperbacks</a>, or <a href="/wiki/Graphic_novel" title="Graphic novel">graphic novels</a>).\n</p><p>Authors such as <a href="/wiki/Miguel_de_Cervantes" title="Miguel de Cervantes">Miguel de Cervantes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alexandre_Dumas" title="Alexandre Dumas">Alexandre Dumas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charles_Dickens" title="Charles Dickens">Charles Dickens</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jane_Austen" title="Jane Austen">Jane Austen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Conan_Doyle" title="Arthur Conan Doyle">Arthur Conan Doyle</a>, <a href="/wiki/Victor_Hugo" title="Victor Hugo">Victor Hugo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jules_Verne" title="Jules Verne">Jules Verne</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rick_Riordan" title="Rick Riordan">Rick Riordan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jack_Higgins" title="Jack Higgins">Jack Higgins</a> and <a href="/wiki/Leon_Uris" title="Leon Uris">Leon Uris</a> have not been included in the table because no exact figures could be found\xe2\x80\x94although there are indications that they too have more than 100 million copies of their work in print.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources.
