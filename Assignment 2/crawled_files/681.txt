and <a href="/wiki/Anna_Soubry" title="Anna Soubry">Anna Soubry</a> MP (<a href="/wiki/Conservative_Party_(UK)" title="Conservative Party (UK)">Conservative</a>); the remaining members of the group (GCG) representing more than 500,000 members opposed to a hard Brexit had formed, with Umunna as its leader.<sup id="cite_ref-mason_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mason-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Later that month it was reported that <a href="/wiki/George_Soros" title="George Soros">George Soros</a>\'s <a href="/wiki/Open_Society_Foundations" \xc2\xa335,000 to <a href="/wiki/Scientists_for_EU" class="mw-redirect" title="Scientists for EU">Scientists for EU</a>, two of the grassroot groups.<sup id="cite_ref-ft.com_8-0" class="reference"><a This was also reported to be in order to work alongside the APPG on EU Relations. Umunna commented, "In our democracy, it is vital that the people get their say on Brexit, rather than their elected representatives in Parliament being reduced to some rubber stamp for whatever plan <a href="/wiki/Boris_Johnson" title="Boris Johnson">Boris Johnson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Rees-Mogg" title="Jacob Rees-Mogg">Jacob Rees-Mogg</a> and <a href="/wiki/Michael_Gove" title="Michael Gove">Michael Gove</a> have been putting together behind closed doors".<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h3><span id="People.27s_Vote_formation"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="People\'s_Vote_formation">People\'s Vote formation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=People%27s_Vote&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: People&#039;s Vote formation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>\n<p>People\'s Vote was launched at an event in London on 15 April 2018, at <a href="/wiki/Electric_Ballroom" title="Electric Ballroom">The Electric Ballroom</a> in Camden.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> The event comprised <a href="/wiki/Andy_Parsons" title="Andy Parsons">Andy Parsons</a> with MPs Umunna, Soubry, <a href="/wiki/Layla_Moran" title="Layla Moran">Layla Moran</a> (Liberal Democrats) and Lucas speaking, as well as actor <a href="/wiki/Patrick_Stewart" title="Patrick Stewart">Patrick Stewart</a>. Speakers included actor <a href="/wiki/Tony_Robinson" title="Tony Robinson">Tony Robinson</a> (who criticised the Labour leader <a href="/wiki/Jeremy_Corbyn" title="Jeremy Corbyn">Jeremy Corbyn</a> for not attending the march),<sup id="cite_ref-Sky_100,000_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Sky_100,000-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a
