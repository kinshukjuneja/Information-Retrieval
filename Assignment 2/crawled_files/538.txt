lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>This is a <b>list of islands of the Isle of Man</b>. The Isle of Man (<a href="/wiki/Manx_language" title="Manx language">Manx</a>: <i lang="gv">Ellan Vannin</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Crown_dependency" class="mw-redirect" title="Crown dependency">Crown dependency</a> in the centre of the <a href="/wiki/Irish_Sea" title="Irish Sea">Irish Sea</a>.  It is not part of the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a> or <a href="/wiki/European_Union" title="European Union">European Union</a> or a member of the <a href="/wiki/United_Nations" title="United Nations">United Nations</a>.\n</p><p>Several islands in the <a href="/wiki/Irish_Sea" title="Irish Sea">Irish Sea</a> are part of Crown dependency of the <a href="/wiki/Isle_of_Man" title="Isle of Man">Isle of Man</a>.
