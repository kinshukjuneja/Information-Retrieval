href="/wiki/ICAO_airport_code" title="ICAO airport code">ICAO</a>: <b>EGHK</b>) was located 0.6&#160;<a href="/wiki/Nautical_mile" title="Nautical mile">NM</a> (1.1&#160;km; 0.69&#160;mi) northeast of <a href="/wiki/Penzance" title="Penzance">Penzance</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cornwall" title="Cornwall">Cornwall</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-aip_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-aip-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The heliport hosted scheduled flights to the <a href="/wiki/Isles_of_Scilly" title="Isles of Scilly">Isles of Scilly</a>, with a connection to the railway network at <a href="/wiki/Penzance_railway_station" title="Penzance railway station">Penzance railway station</a> by a special bus service. The heliport had a single concrete landing pad, which was 30&#160;m \xc3\x97&#160;30&#160;m (98&#160;ft \xc3\x97&#160;98&#160;ft), inside a 373&#160;m \xc3\x97&#160;45&#160;m (1,224&#160;ft \xc3\x97&#160;148&#160;ft) grass strip, 08/26.\n</p><p>The heliport was officially closed on 31 October 2012. A new heliport is now under construction which will be the base for a new helicopter service to the islands of Tresco and St Mary\'s.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<div id="toc" class="toc"><input type="checkbox" role="button" id="toctogglecheckbox" class="toctogglecheckbox" style="display:none" /><div class="toctitle" lang="en" dir="ltr"><h2>Contents</h2><span class="toctogglespan"><label class="toctogglelabel" for="toctogglecheckbox"></label></span></div>\n<ul>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-1"><a href="#History"><span class="tocnumber">1</span> <span class="toctext">History</span></a></li>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-2"><a href="#New_Heliport"><span class="tocnumber">2</span> <span class="toctext">New Heliport</span></a></li>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-3"><a href="#References"><span class="tocnumber">3</span> <span class="toctext">References</span></a></li>\n<li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-4"><a href="#External_links"><span class="tocnumber">4</span> <span class="toctext">External links</span></a></li>\n</ul>\n</div>\n\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Penzance_Heliport&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>Services started on 1 May 1964 when <a href="/wiki/British_Airways_Helicopters" title="British Airways Helicopters">BEA Helicopters Limited</a> operated the first service between Penzance and the <a href="/wiki/Isles_of_Scilly" title="Isles of Scilly">Isles of Scilly</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Sikorsky_S-61" title="Sikorsky S-61">Sikorsky S-61</a>. The construction of the heliport cost \xc2\xa388,000<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> (equivalent to \xc2\xa31,600,000&#32;in 2016).<sup id="cite_ref-inflation-UK_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-inflation-UK-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>The heliport was opened formally on 1 September 1964 by Councillor Alfred Beckerleg, the Mayor of Penzance with the Lady Mayoress.\n</p><p>Due to the high costs of maintaining the service and falling passenger numbers, <a href="/wiki/British_International_Helicopters" title="British International Helicopters">British International Helicopters</a> ceased the operation of the helicopter route on 31 October 2012.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> The company announced that it intended to sell the Heliport to a supermarket chain<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and relocate to another site in West Cornwall to raise funds, although no suitable alternative site was found in time to save the service.\n</p><p>In early April 2013, demolition work on the main terminal buildings began, and by September 2013, nothing remains of the former heliport, with a supermarket chain now dominating the site.\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="New_Heliport">New Heliport</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Penzance_Heliport&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: New Heliport">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>In 2016 plans were announced for a new helicopter service between Penzance and the Isles of Scilly, serving both St Mary\'s and Tresco. The proposals include building a new heliport on Jelbert Way, Penzance, near to the location of the old one.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> The planning application received the highest level of support Cornwall Council had ever received for a planning application.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>Planning consent for the new heliport was first granted in a unanimous decision by Cornwall Council\'s Strategic Planning Committee in February 2017.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> The decision was later challenged by the Isles of Scilly Steamship Company in a Judicial Review<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>. A petition against the Judicial Review gained more than 11,000 signatures<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> and an amended planning application was submitted.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> \n</p><p>The amended application was granted planning consent in a second unanimous decision by Cornwall Council in August 2018.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>The new heliport will be built during 2018 and 2019, with services expected to commence in 2019 or 2020, flying to the islands of St Mary\'s and Tresco.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="References">References</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Penzance_Heliport&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: References">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<div class="reflist" style="list-style-type: decimal;">\n<div class="mw-references-wrap mw-references-columns"><ol class="references">\n<li id="cite_note-aip-1"><span class="mw-cite-backlink">^ <a href="#cite_ref-aip_1-0"><sup><i><b>a</b></i></sup></a> <a href="#cite_ref-aip_1-1"><sup><i><b>b</b></i></sup></a></span> <span class="reference-text"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.nats-uk.ead-it.com/public/index.php%3Foption=com_content&amp;task=blogcategory&amp;id=174&amp;Itemid=65.html">Penzance - EGHK</a></span>\n</li>\n<li id="cite_note-2"><span class="mw-cite-backlink"><b><a href="#cite_ref-2">^</a></b></span> <span class="reference-text"><cite class="citation web"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://penzanceheliport.co.uk/">"Isles of Scilly Helicopter from Penzance Heliport"</a>. <i>Penzance Heliport</i><span class="reference-accessdate">.
