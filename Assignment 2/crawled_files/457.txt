The events described by the memories may date back hundreds, thousands, or even tens of thousands of years and often have a local significance. They may explain physical features in the local <a href="/wiki/Environment_(biophysical)" class="mw-redirect" title="Environment (biophysical)">environment</a>, provide reasons for <a href="/wiki/Cultural" class="mw-redirect" title="Cultural">cultural</a> traditions or give <a href="/wiki/Etymologies" class="mw-redirect" title="Etymologies">etymologies</a> for the names of local
