The regiment saw action at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Trenton" title="Battle of Trenton">Battle of Trenton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Princeton" title="Battle of Princeton">Battle of Princeton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Brandywine" title="Battle of Brandywine">Battle of Brandywine</a>, <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Germantown" title="Battle of Germantown">Battle of Germantown</a>, <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Monmouth" title="Battle of Monmouth">Battle of Monmouth</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Charleston" title="Siege of Charleston">Siege of Charleston</a>.
