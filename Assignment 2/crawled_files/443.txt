An original installment has appeared on nearly every succeeding <a href="/wiki/Nintendo_video_game_console" class="mw-redirect" title="Nintendo video game console">Nintendo video game console</a> with the exception of the <a href="/wiki/Game_Boy_Color" title="Game Boy Color">Game Boy Color</a> since its debut until 2004. <i>GP Legend</i> marks the first time the franchise has hit a gaming system twice in its lifetime in the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-IGN_GP_Legend_review_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-IGN_GP_Legend_review-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The 2004 release of <i>Climax</i> was the last video game in the franchise before its hiatus.<sup id="cite_ref-localization_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-localization-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-about_a_decade_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-about_a_decade-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Since then, multiple video games were re-released through Nintendo\'s <a href="/wiki/Digital_distribution" title="Digital distribution">digital distribution</a> channels. The series currently includes eight released video games, a <a href="/wiki/Television_program" class="mw-redirect" title="Television program">television series</a>, and video game soundtracks released on audio <a href="/wiki/Compact_Disc" class="mw-redirect" title="Compact Disc">CDs</a>.\n</p><p><a href="/wiki/Gameplay" title="Gameplay">Gameplay</a> consists of racing in futuristic hovercrafts and using their speed-boosting abilities to navigate through the courses as quickly as possible in settings like the recurring Mute City, Big Blue and Port Town. The first game was labeled by critics as an influential video game that created the futuristic racing <a href="/wiki/Genre" title="Genre">subgenre</a><sup id="cite_ref-cvgReview_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cvgReview-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-top91_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-top91-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> as well as inspired the creation of numerous racing games such as <a href="/wiki/Daytona_USA_(arcade_game)" class="mw-redirect" title="Daytona USA (arcade game)"><i>Daytona USA</i></a> and the <a href="/wiki/Wipeout_(video_game_series)" class="mw-redirect" title="Wipeout (video game series)"><i>Wipeout</i></a> series.<sup id="cite_ref-AC_GC_interview_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AC_GC_interview-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-VC_BS_review_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-VC_BS_review-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> The series casually centers around the F-Zero racer <a href="/wiki/Captain_Falcon" title="Captain Falcon">Captain Falcon</a> and his talented racing and bounty hunting abilities as well as his encounters with the other <i>F-Zero</i> characters.<sup id="cite_ref-Capt_Falcon_IGN_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Capt_Falcon_IGN-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-GS_GX_review_10-0" class="reference"><a
