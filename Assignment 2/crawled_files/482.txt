one of six <a href="/wiki/Division_(sport)" title="Division (sport)">divisions</a> in <a href="/wiki/Major_League_Baseball" title="Major League Baseball">Major League Baseball</a>. This division was formed in the realignment of 1994, and  its teams are all located in the <a href="/wiki/Midwestern_United_States" title="Midwestern United States">Midwestern United States</a>. The Central is currently the only division in the Major Leagues in which all of its members have won a <a href="/wiki/World_Series" title="World Series">World Series</a> title. The <a href="/wiki/2006_Detroit_Tigers_season" title="2006 Detroit Tigers season">2006 Detroit Tigers</a> were the first team from the Central to qualify as the wild card. MLB revamped the postseason starting in <a href="/wiki/2012_Major_League_Baseball_season" title="2012 Major League Baseball season">2012</a>, creating a new single-game playoff where two wildcards competed against each other while the division winners each received a bye. The winner of the American League <a href="/wiki/Major_League_Baseball_Wild_Card_game" class="mw-redirect" title="Major League Baseball Wild Card game">wild card game</a> moves on to face the top-seeded team of the AL in the <a href="/wiki/American_League_Division_Series" title="American League Division Series">American League Division Series</a>. In <a href="/wiki/2013_Major_League_Baseball_season" title="2013 Major League Baseball season">2013</a>, the Indians became the first team from the AL Central to qualify as a wild card under the new postseason format. In <a href="/wiki/2014_Major_League_Baseball_season" title="2014 Major League Baseball season">2014</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Kansas_City_Royals" title="Kansas City Royals">Kansas City Royals</a> ended a 29-year postseason drought returning to the playoffs for the first time since winning the World Series in <a href="/wiki/1985_Major_League_Baseball_season" title="1985 Major League Baseball season">1985</a>.\n</p>\n<table class="wikitable sortable">\n<tbody><tr>\n<th style="background: #e3e3e3;">Year\n</th>\n<th style="background: #e3e3e3;">Winner\n</th>\n<th style="background:
