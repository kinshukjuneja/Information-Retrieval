Throughout his career, he  used the pseudonyms Andrew Garve, Roger Bax and Paul Somers.\n</p><p>He was a founder-member of the <a href="/wiki/Crime_Writers%27_Association" title="Crime Writers&#39; Association">Crime Writers\' Association</a> in 1953 and, with <a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Ferrars" title="Elizabeth Ferrars">Elizabeth Ferrars</a>, its first joint secretary.\n</p>\n<div id="toc"
