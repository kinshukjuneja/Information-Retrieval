class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>\n\t\t\t\t<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>\nThe  <b>Winter Meetings</b> are an annual event, held each December, in which representatives of all 30 <a href="/wiki/Major_League_Baseball" title="Major League Baseball">Major League Baseball</a> teams and their 160 <a href="/wiki/Minor_league_baseball" class="mw-redirect" title="Minor league baseball">minor league baseball</a> affiliates convene for four days to discuss league business and conduct off-season trades and transactions. Attendees include league executives, team owners, general managers, team scouts, visitors from baseball-playing countries, trade show exhibitors, and people seeking employment with minor league organizations.<sup id="cite_ref-FOOTNOTEPeden2011195_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FOOTNOTEPeden2011195-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Rule_5_draft" title="Rule 5 draft">Rule 5 draft</a>, in which minor league players who are not on a team\'s <a href="/wiki/40-man_roster" class="mw-redirect" title="40-man roster">40-man roster</a> can be drafted by a major league team, is held on the last day of the meetings.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>The 2017 Winter Meetings were held from December 9 to 13 at the Swan and Dolphin resort in Orlando, Florida. <a href="/wiki/Mandalay_Bay" title="Mandalay Bay">Mandalay Bay</a> Resort in Las Vegas will host in 2018.\n</p>\n<div id="toc" class="toc"><input type="checkbox" role="button" (January 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> The Winter Meetings became an annual event in 1901.\n</p>\n<div class="div-col columns column-width" style="-moz-column-width: 15em; href="/w/index.php?title=Winter_Meetings&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Attendees">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The Winter Meetings attract several thousand participants; in 2014 organizers anticipated 3,000 attendees. These include team owners, field managers, team scouts, players\' agents, lawyers and accountants specializing in baseball, and visitors from baseball-playing countries.<sup id="cite_ref-FOOTNOTEPeden2011195_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FOOTNOTEPeden2011195-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FOOTNOTESolomonFreeman2012185_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FOOTNOTESolomonFreeman2012185-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FOOTNOTEGorman20077\xe2\x80\x938_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FOOTNOTEGorman20077\xe2\x80\x938-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Baseball players generally do not attend, although <a href="/wiki/Free_agent" title="Free agent">free agents</a> come to introduce themselves to many teams.<sup id="cite_ref-FOOTNOTESolomonFreeman2012185_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FOOTNOTESolomonFreeman2012185-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> At the 2014 Winter Meetings in San Diego, an estimated 750 media personnel received <a href="/wiki/Press_pass" title="Press pass">press passes</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-wsj_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wsj-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>Receptions are held nightly by each of the 30 major league teams for their minor league affiliates.<sup id="cite_ref-FOOTNOTESolomonFreeman2012182_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FOOTNOTESolomonFreeman2012182-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> A luncheon is also held for major league managers and baseball reporters.<sup id="cite_ref-nyt_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyt-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Player_trades_and_signings">Player trades and signings</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Winter_Meetings&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Player trades and signings">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:152px;"><a href="/wiki/File:BarryBonds1993.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5e/BarryBonds1993.jpg/150px-BarryBonds1993.jpg" width="150" height="199" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5e/BarryBonds1993.jpg/225px-BarryBonds1993.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5e/BarryBonds1993.jpg/300px-BarryBonds1993.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1014" data-file-height="1342" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:BarryBonds1993.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Barry_Bonds" title="Barry Bonds">Barry Bonds</a> playing for the <a href="/wiki/San_Francisco_Giants" title="San Francisco Giants">San Francisco Giants</a> in 1993</div></div></div>\n<p>With all the principals in one place, the Winter Meetings are typically the site of player trades and free-agent signings.<sup id="cite_ref-FOOTNOTECarroll200778_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FOOTNOTECarroll200778-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> However, the informal meetings that used to take place in hotel lobbies up until the end of the 20th century have been replaced by texting and emailing; most interactions take place in the privacy of suites due to the preponderance of media personnel and fans converging on the site.<sup id="cite_ref-wsj_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wsj-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-nyt_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyt-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>Among the notable trades and signings that have been made at the Winter Meetings are:\n</p>\n<ul><li>At the 1975 Winter Meetings in Fort Lauderdale, new <a href="/wiki/Chicago_White_Sox" title="Chicago White Sox">Chicago White Sox</a> owner <a href="/wiki/Bill_Veeck" title="Bill Veeck">Bill Veeck</a> sat at a table in the lobby behind a sign that said "Open for Business". During the course of the meetings, Veeck negotiated six trades involving 22 players.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup></li>\n<li>At the 1988 Winter Meetings in Atlanta, the <a href="/wiki/Texas_Rangers_(baseball)" title="Texas Rangers (baseball)">Texas Rangers</a> closed three trades involving 15 players and signed free agent pitcher <a href="/wiki/Nolan_Ryan" title="Nolan Ryan">Nolan Ryan</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FOOTNOTESypher1990100_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FOOTNOTESypher1990100-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup></li>\n<li>At the 1992 Winter Meetings in Louisville, first-time free agent <a href="/wiki/Barry_Bonds" title="Barry Bonds">Barry Bonds</a> was signed by the <a href="/wiki/San_Francisco_Giants" title="San Francisco Giants">San Francisco Giants</a> for six years and $43 million. Bonds personally negotiated to have a hotel suite at his disposal during road games.<sup id="cite_ref-nyt_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyt-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li>\n<li>On the last day of the 2011 Winter Meetings in Dallas, <a href="/wiki/Albert_Pujols" title="Albert Pujols">Albert Pujols</a>, who had won a <a href="/wiki/World_Series" title="World Series">World Series</a> ring with the <a href="/wiki/St._Louis_Cardinals" title="St. Louis Cardinals">St. Louis Cardinals</a> that fall, inked a 10-year, $250 million deal with the <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles_Angels" title="Los Angeles Angels">Los Angeles Angels</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup></li>\n<li>In the space of 24 hours at the 2014 Winter Meetings in San Diego, the <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles_Dodgers" title="Los Angeles Dodgers">Los Angeles Dodgers</a> concluded six transactions with four teams, involving 19 players and a free agent.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Other_events">Other events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Winter_Meetings&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Other events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:152px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Pants-rowland.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" data-file-height="393" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Pants-rowland.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Pants_Rowland" title="Pants Rowland">Pants Rowland</a>, winner of the first "<a href="/wiki/King_of_Baseball" title="King of Baseball">King of Baseball</a>" award in 1951.</div></div></div>\n<p>Concurrent with the Winter Meetings, a trade show featuring close to 300 vendors of baseball equipment, services, and promotions takes place.<sup id="cite_ref-FOOTNOTESolomonFreeman2012185_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FOOTNOTESolomonFreeman2012185-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Another annual event is the Professional Baseball Employment Opportunities Job Fair, during which recent college graduates seeking internships and employment with minor league organizations schedule on-site interviews.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> The month of December is considered "the height of baseball hiring season", as 400 to 500 workers are hired each year.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>Since 1951, the "<a href="/wiki/King_of_Baseball" title="King of Baseball">King of Baseball</a>" title has been awarded to a minor league veteran at the Winter Meetings banquet.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>Several events associated with the <a href="/wiki/National_Baseball_Hall_of_Fame_and_Museum" title="National Baseball Hall of Fame and Museum">Hall of Fame</a> also take place at the Winter Meetings:\n</p>\n<ul><li>The voting bodies that superseded the <a href="/wiki/Veterans_Committee" title="Veterans Committee">Veterans Committee</a>, which are now the only bodies that elect long-retired players and non-playing personnel, meet and vote.<sup id="cite_ref-nyt_9-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyt-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li>\n<li>The winner of the <a href="/wiki/Ford_C._Frick_Award" title="Ford C. Frick Award">Ford C. Frick Award</a> for excellence in broadcasting is announced.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup></li>\n<li>The <a href="/wiki/Baseball_Writers%27_Association_of_America" title="Baseball Writers&#39; Association of America">Baseball Writers\' Association of America</a> conducts its annual meeting<sup id="cite_ref-nyt_9-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyt-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> and announces the recipient of its <a href="/wiki/J._G._Taylor_Spink_Award" title="J. G. Taylor Spink Award">J. G. Taylor Spink Award</a> for excellence in baseball writing.<sup id="cite_ref-FOOTNOTEGeltner2012203_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FOOTNOTEGeltner2012203-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> Both the Frick and Spink Awards are presented as part of the Hall of Fame\'s annual induction festivities.</li>\n<li>The Scout of the Year award is presented at a special banquet.<sup id="cite_ref-sun_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-sun-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FOOTNOTESandovalNowlin2011165_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FOOTNOTESandovalNowlin2011165-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notes">Notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Winter_Meetings&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Notes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="References">References</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Winter_Meetings&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: References">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>\n<div class="reflist columns references-column-width" style="-moz-column-width: 30em;
