Utrecht">UVV Utrecht</a></td></tr></tbody></table>\n<p>The <b>Honkbal Overgangsklasse</b> (<a href="/wiki/Dutch_language" title="Dutch language">Dutch</a> for <i>Baseball Transition League</i>) is the second highest level of <a href="/wiki/Professional_baseball" title="Professional baseball">professional baseball</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Baseball_in_the_Netherlands" title="Baseball in the Netherlands">Netherlands</a>. It is a twelve-team <a href="/wiki/Sports_league" title="Sports league">league</a> that plays a 22-game schedule followed by two separate 15-game schedules for the best six teams and the weakest six teams, and is overseen by the <a href="/wiki/Royal_Netherlands_Baseball_and_Softball_Federation" class="mw-redirect" title="Royal Netherlands Baseball and Softball Federation">KNBSB</a>. Games are played principally on weekends. The season runs from April to August and is followed by a <a href="/wiki/Promotion_and_relegation" title="Promotion and relegation">promotion and relegation</a> system with the highest level of baseball in the Netherlands, the <a href="/wiki/Honkbal_Hoofdklasse" title="Honkbal Hoofdklasse">Honkbal Hoofdklasse</a>, so that the composition of the top level may change from year to year.\n</p>\n<div id="toc" class="toc"><input type="checkbox" role="button" id="toctogglecheckbox" class="toctogglecheckbox" style="display:none" /><div class="toctitle" lang="en"
