As a result, the Duchy was obliged to negotiate an agreement rather than impose unilaterally its own scheme.\n</p><p>Over the years, membership has more than tripled, and the association has taken up a range of issues on Scilly and kept members alert to <a href="/wiki/Duchy" title="Duchy">Duchy</a> plans for the Islands. It represents business, farm and residential tenants of the Duchy of Cornwall in Britain\xe2\x80\x99s most south-westerly community. It engages in both dialogue and dispute with the Duchy.\n</p><p>The DTA produces and circulates four <a href="/wiki/Newsletter" title="Newsletter">newsletters</a> each year.
