class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Wagstaff_Edit.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Wagstaff on the <a href="/wiki/Hugh_Town" title="Hugh Town">Hugh Town</a> quay on <a href="/wiki/St_Mary%27s,_Isles_of_Scilly" title="St Mary&#39;s, Isles of Scilly">St Mary\'s, Isles of Scilly</a>, 2010</div></div></div>\n<p><b>William Wagstaff</b>, commonly known as <b>Will Wagstaff</b>, is a leading <a href="/wiki/Ornithologist" class="mw-redirect" title="Ornithologist">ornithologist</a> and <a href="/wiki/Naturalist" class="mw-redirect" title="Naturalist">naturalist</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Isles_of_Scilly" title="Isles of Scilly">Isles of Scilly</a>, and also an author. His popular guided wildlife walks<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> have made him both a well-known and popular figure in the islands.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Originally from South Wales, Wagstaff has lived on the <a href="/wiki/Isles_of_Scilly" title="Isles of Scilly">Isles of Scilly</a> since 1981. He has had an active role in conservation work around the islands for more than 20 years, and has led guided wildlife walks there since 1985.<sup id="cite_ref-islandwildlifetours.co.uk_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-islandwildlifetours.co.uk-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He is currently Honorary President and Chairman of the Isles of Scilly Bird Group<sup id="cite_ref-islandwildlifetours.co.uk_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-islandwildlifetours.co.uk-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and regularly presents slideshows and leads other events on the islands. He also writes a regular column <i>A Walk on the Wild Side</i> for the local magazine <a href="/wiki/Scilly_Now_%26_Then" title="Scilly Now &amp; Then">Scilly Now &amp; Then</a>.  He is a Tour Leader for Island Holidays<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and runs the Island Wildlife Tours group.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He is part of the Travelling Naturalist group.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>Wagstaff also works in other parts of the world, such as <a href="/wiki/Florida" title="Florida">Florida</a><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> and the <a href="/wiki/Falkland_Islands" title="Falkland Islands">Falkland Islands</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a
